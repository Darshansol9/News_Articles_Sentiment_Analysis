The deer took off -- or so she thought.As the driver, Ellen Snyder, opened the door to check out the damage to her SUV, the deer suddenly charged back across the road towards her vehicle, pinning her inside."It was trying to get in. I don't know exactly why," Snyder told CNN affiliate News 12 New Jersey .
