(CNN) And the crowd goes wild -- over "sticker kid"?A Drake University freshman whose face was covered with stickers may have stolen the spotlight from Democratic presidential candidate Hillary Clinton as she gave her Iowa caucus speech on Monday night.Eighteen-year-old Peter Clinkscales ' dancing, dabbing and his attempt to eat the stickers plastered on his cheeks took Twitter by storm.To viewers following Clinton's campaign speech on Twitter, he became known as #Stickerboy, #StickerKid, or #Stickerface.Clinkscales told CNN's Jeanne Moos that when he noticed the cameras were on Clinton and he was probably visible in the background, the only idea that came to his mind was to "goof around."
