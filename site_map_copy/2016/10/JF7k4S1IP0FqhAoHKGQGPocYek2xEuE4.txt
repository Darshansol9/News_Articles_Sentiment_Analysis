Story highlights Two officers were shot after responding to 911 callThe suspect is still at large(CNN) When Chrystal King set off on a grocery store errand, little did she know she'd end up saving the lives of two Oklahoma police officers.On Sunday, the Wellston officers were responding to a shots fired call when a gunman at the scene began spraying the area with bullets. Both officers were hit; one in the foot, the other in the leg.On her drive home from the store, King spotted the two bloodied officers alongside the road. Without hesitation, she sprung to action."Both of them were bleeding out their legs pretty bad," King told CNN affiliate KFOR . "I got some old shirts out of the truck and tied them up."King recalls one officer's gratitude. "He said 'Thank you, thank you.' Kept telling me 'Thank you.'"Read More
