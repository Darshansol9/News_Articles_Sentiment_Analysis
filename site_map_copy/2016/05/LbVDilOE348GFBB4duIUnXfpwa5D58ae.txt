(CNN) North Carolina will meet a Monday deadline to respond to the U.S. Justice Department about the state's transgender bathroom law, Gov. Pat McCrory said Thursday."We're reviewing (the Justice Department letter) and we hope to have a response in the very near future." he said, according to CNN affiliate WNCN.When asked if the state will respond before Monday's deadline set by the Justice Department, McCrory answered in the affirmative."Definitely," he said.
