(CNN) Man's best friend gets a second chance at life; thanks to an extern.Ollie, a 10-year-old Sheltie from Portland, Oregon, was moments from being euthanized when a veterinary extern spotted the problem that left the dog fighting for his life.The dog's owners had a tough decision to make when his health started to rapidly deteriorate.
