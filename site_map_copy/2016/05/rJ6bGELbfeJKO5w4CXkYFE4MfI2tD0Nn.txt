Story highlights The bees reportedly sting several children and even kill two dogsA beekeper is brought in to contain the hoard(CNN) A scourge of killer bees terrorized a California neighborhood over the weekend, attacking people and pets before being contained by what had to be a very brave beekeeper.The Concord Police Department issued an alert after they received calls about roving aggressive bees in the neighborhood.The Hitchcockian scene originated on Concord's Hitchcock Drive, where police say an amateur beekeeper accidentally loosed the swarm on Saturday.The pests reportedly stung several children and even killed two dogs.
