Story highlights Turner Sports reporter Craig Sager died Thursday at 65Such figures as Hank Aaron, Isaiah Thomas and Keith Olbermann paid tribute(CNN) Craig Sager was more than just a courtside reporter in flashy suits to his colleagues and to the athletes and coaches he interviewed for more than two decades.NBA Commissioner Adam Silver said: "Craig was as vital to the NBA as the players and coaches. ... . Craig earned widespread respect for his insightful reporting and inspired so many most recently with his courage."His Turner Sports coworker Greg Anthony tweeted: "There is just no better person, colleague and friend... my thoughts and prayers are with his family and our Turner sports family."Sager was a fixture on national NBA broadcasts, part of his 40-plus year career that also included working at CNN (owned by Turner Broadcasting) and covered major sporting events like the World Cup and the Olympics.
