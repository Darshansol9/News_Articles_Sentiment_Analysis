Story highlights Hundreds of sea lions scouting for smelt have been on the Columbia River dock for weeks.The number of sea lions is becoming a problem for the city.(CNN) This small city in Oregon has a sea lion problem.Hundreds of sea lions scouting the Columbia River for smelt have overtaken a dock in Rainier, and the number of sea lions keeps growing.Residents have mixed reactions to their new neighbors. Some love their presence."This is amazing! I heard it was like this and I'm hard of hearing but I could hear them up there on the main drag," Tina Allerdings, who was at the docks to visit the sea lions, told a CNN affiliate.However, others, particularly the city's fishermen, are worried the sea lions may be damaging the wooden docks.
