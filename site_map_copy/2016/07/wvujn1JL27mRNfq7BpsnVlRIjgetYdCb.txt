His two older sisters survived.While in a French orphanage, Wiesel's picture was taken by a journalist who came to visit. One of his sisters saw the picture in the paper and they were reunited. Later he was reunited with his other sister.Wiesel appeared in a famous photograph taken at Buchenwald of male prisoners in bunk beds.Early in his career, Wiesel wrote for French newspaper L'Arche, Tel Aviv newspaper Yedioth Ahronoth, and New York City newspaper Jewish Daily Forward.Lost his life savings in Bernard Madoff 's Ponzi scheme.
