(CNN) People gathered at a memorial outside the Dallas Police Department were taken by surprise on Sunday when a man pulled up on a bicycle, a piano in tow. Without saying a word, the gentleman began playing a rendition of John Lennon's "Imagine."A witness told CNN the mystery pianist left without saying a word, refusing to speak to reporters as he cycled away.
