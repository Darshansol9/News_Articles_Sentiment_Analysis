Story highlights Emergency responders tranquilized Zeya, a 4-year-old Amur leopardNo injuries were reported from her escape(CNN) A 4-year-old leopard escaped from an exhibit at Salt Lake City's Hogle Zoo on Tuesday morning, a zoo official said."Per our safety protocol, our staff jumped into action and escorted all guests into indoor buildings," community relations manager Erica Hansen said.Emergency teams found the Amur leopard, named Zeya, and tranquilized her, Hansen said. Zeya was taken to a holding area.Hansen said it was not immediately clear how Zeya managed to dart out.
