Story highlights National Security Advisor Susan Rice: Advances are being made in fight against ISISKey cities of Raqqa and Mosul will fall to US-backed Syrian forces, Rice says(CNN) Coalition forces are making progress in the battle against ISIS, National Security Advisor Susan Rice said Sunday.In an interview with CNN's Fareed Zakaria, Rice said she believes the key cities Mosul in Iraq and Raqqa in Syria would fall to US-backed Syrian forces."The Iraqi Security Forces inside of Iraq have taken back about 55 percent of the populated territory that ISIL originally seized back in 2014," Rice said, using another name for the Islamic State.
