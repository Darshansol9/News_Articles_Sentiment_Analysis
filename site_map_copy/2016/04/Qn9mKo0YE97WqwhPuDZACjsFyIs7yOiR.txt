They still await his revelations and follow his directives, both difficult and bizarre.But some members are leaving the Fundamentalist Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints -- and disobeying their prophet's longstanding orders to avoid law enforcement and "answer them nothing."They include the former cooks and drivers for the Jeffs family, as well as ex-wives and others who hovered close to the church leaders and their power center.
