(CNN) A grand jury for the Will Smith murder investigation will be convened Thursday, John Fuller, attorney for the accused killer Cardell Hayes tells CNN."Witnesses are definitely subpoenaed for the grand jury that will be convened tomorrow," he said Wednesday. "They are served to testify tomorrow."Smith, a former New Orleans Saints defensive end, was gunned down following an altercation which occurred as a result of a traffic crash Saturday April 9. Suspect Cardell Hayes, 28, is accused of killing him after they "exchanged words" in the street.His wife, Racquel, was hospitalized with a gunshot wound to the leg.The next court appearance for Hayes, who's been charged with second-degree murder, is set for April 28. Hayes' attorney argues he fired in self defense and is "legally not guilty."
