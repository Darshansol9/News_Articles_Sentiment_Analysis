Story highlights Three students have been suspended following the death in a fight of a 16-year-oldThe cause of the fatal fight remains under investigationNo charges have been filed(CNN) Delaware school officials say three students have been indefinitely suspended following the death of 16-year-old girl in a bathroom fight last Thursday.The three students from Howard High School of Technology in Wilmington, Delaware, were questioned by police after the fight and "have not been back in school since the incident," New Castle School District spokeswoman Kathy Demarest told CNN.Wilmington Police Chief Bobby Cummings was part of a Town Hall of parents and teachers Monday night, and said investigators are looking at three individuals. Authorities are continuing their investigation and so far have not filed any charges.The New Castle School District identified the dead girl as Amy Inita Joyner-Francis.An autopsy was scheduled to determine the cause of death.Read More
