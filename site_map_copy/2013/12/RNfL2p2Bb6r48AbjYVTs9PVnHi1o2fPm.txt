More than 100 law officers continued a nationwide manhunt for an armed bank robber suspected of shooting two Tupelo, Mississippi, police officers, one of them fatally, the FBI said Thursday.Police are looking for this dark gray Dodge Stratus, which is believed to be the getaway vehicle.The bank robbery in Tupelo turned into a shootout Monday afternoon, and the robber or robbers escaped and remain at large, the FBI said.The FBI, local banks and business are offering a more than $150,000 reward leading to the identification and capture of the bank robber, the FBI said.The suspect was driving a gray sedan as he fled eastbound on Carnation Street in Tupelo, the FBI said.
