CNN Films' "Girl Rising" documents extraordinary girls and the power of education to change the world. Watch June 16 on CNNBy Betsy Anderson, CNN(CNN) -- Eulalia goes to school on a motorcycle.The 10-year-old girl lives in the Puno region of Peru with her parents and six siblings. There is no school near Eulalia's home, so on Mondays, her father gives her a ride down the mountain on his motorcycle to a boarding school run by the humanitarian organization CARE. She attends school during the week and comes home on the weekends.For Eulalia, this ride to school is a journey into a promising future that is hard to come by in Peru. She is one of nearly a million indigenous children who struggle to get an education.Read More
