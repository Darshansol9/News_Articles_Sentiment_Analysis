(CNNMoney)-- Doctors are still mostly men, and nurses are almost all women. But pharmacists are another story.Pharmacists are a fast-growing profession offering a six-figure salary -- and the pay is nearly equal for men and women."The position of pharmacist is probably the most egalitarian of all U.S. professions today," Harvard economists Claudia Goldin and Lawrence Katz wrote in a paper on the subject they published in September.Women make up slightly more than 50% of all full-time pharmacists, according to Census data collected in 2011.
