Bond Bridge -- which carries Interstates 35 and 29 over the Missouri River -- and a male followed her, Kansas City police and fire officials said.Some witnesses said a child also went over the side of the bridge, officials said.The Kansas City Fire Department received a call about the incident around 11:30 a.m. CT (12:30 p.m. ET), Kansas City Fire Deputy Chief Jeff Grote said.
