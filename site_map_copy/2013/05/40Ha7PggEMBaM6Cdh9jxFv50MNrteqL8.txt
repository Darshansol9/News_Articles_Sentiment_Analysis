By Alan Silverleib, CNN Congressional ProducerWashington (CNN) -- Advocates for comprehensive immigration reform won their first major legislative victory this week when the Senate Judiciary Committee voted 13-5 to approve the bipartisan "Gang of Eight" plan.If enacted, the measure will create a 13-year path to citizenship for most of the country's 11 million undocumented immigrants.It aims to strengthen border security while raising the cap on visas for high-skilled workers and establishing a new visa program for low-skilled workers on America's farms and elsewhere.Read More
