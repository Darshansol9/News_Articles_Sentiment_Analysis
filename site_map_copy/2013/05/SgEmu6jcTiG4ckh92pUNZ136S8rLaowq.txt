Patients also would undergo background checks.Gov. Pat Quinn has not indicated whether he will sign the bill.Meanwhile, Ohio Attorney General Mike DeWine on Friday certified a petition for a proposed amendment allowing for medical marijuana, the first step in an attempt by the Ohio Rights Group to put the issue to a statewide vote in 2014.
