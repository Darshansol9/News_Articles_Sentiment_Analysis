By Sudip Bhattacharya, CNNWashington (CNN) -- It should have been a happy day for Raymond Jose: He had been accepted to college, with scholarships to help pay for it.But when he told his parents, his mother started to cry."I was puzzled why she was crying after hearing such great news," said Jose, who was to attend Montgomery College in Maryland. "That was when she started to explain to me we were undocumented, that we had overstayed our tourist visas."Jose's family had come to the United States from the Philippines in 2000, when Jose was 9.
