By Moni Basu, CNN(CNN) -- David Grosso, 42, was born and raised in the metropolitan Washington area so it's not tough to see why he's a diehard Washington Redskins fan. Been going to games since he was a boy. Season ticket holder.But Grosso, like so many others, objects to the name and mascot of his favorite team."The term Redskins is a racist and derogatory term," he says.These days, Grosso has the power to do something more than air his opinion. He was elected to the D.C. Council in November, and he plans to introduce a resolution Wednesday to rename the team to the Washington Redtails.
