Story highlights The plane was enroute to Danbury Municipal airportAirport control tower lost communication with the plane shortly before it went downA single engine plane crashed into a field in Connecticut on Sunday, killing the pilot, authorities said.The small plane went down near a Danbury residential neighborhood, and was reported by a woman who heard the crash outside her house, said city Fire Chief Geoffrey Herald.No injuries were reported on the ground, he said.According to the Federal Aviation Administration, the plane was a Cirrus SR 22 enroute to Danbury Municipal Airport.The control tower lost communication with the plane shortly before it went down, said FAA spokesman Jim Peters.
