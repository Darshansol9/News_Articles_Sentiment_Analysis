In America is about the America you may or may not see - a cultural crossroad that exists in every town, city and state. We examine the nuances of what people believe, even when those beliefs are based on perceptions and not necessarily on facts.Sometimes, the candid answers we uncover result in celebrations of lives defined by success, triumph and progress. Sometimes, we explore pain and hurt so deep they should not be forgotten or repeated. And sometimes, we step back and laugh about those oh-so-serious conversations.Now, In America is expanding further with the launch of this blog.Your notion about who you are is tied to your personal experiences; In America is an opportunity for you to see the country from other points of view. Our blog will combine reporting, narratives, images and opinions to help define and contextualize the modern American experience.
