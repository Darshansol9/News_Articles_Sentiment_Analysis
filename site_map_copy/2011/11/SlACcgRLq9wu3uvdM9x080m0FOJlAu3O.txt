By Rafael Romo, Senior Latin American Affairs Editor(CNN) - Joaquin Luna was only 18. The senior at Juarez Lincoln High School in Mission, Texas, dreamed of going to college. But since he was in the country illegally, that was nearly impossible.Luna was quickly losing hope of ever going to college, his family says. The Friday after Thanksgiving, Luna put on a suit, kissed his family members, went into the bathroom and shot himself in the head, according to family members."He didn't see no other way or no other option," his brother Diyra Mendoza told CNN affiliate KGBT.Mendoza found the body after hearing the gunshot.
