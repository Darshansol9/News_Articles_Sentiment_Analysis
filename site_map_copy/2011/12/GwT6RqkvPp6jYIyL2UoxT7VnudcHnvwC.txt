Story highlights The plane crashed in Brazos County in Texas Monday nightThe plane was registered in GeorgiaFive people were killed in a plane crash in central Texas Monday night, authorities said.Authorities were alerted about the plane crash at about 10 p.m. CT in Brazos County, according to the Texas Department of Public Safety.Officers found the plane and the five bodies but could not tell what caused the crash. The identities of the victims were not released.The plane was a Piper, fixed wing single-engine aircraft and was registered in Georgia, authorities said.The National Transportation Safety Board was notified and was scheduled to arrive on Tuesday.
