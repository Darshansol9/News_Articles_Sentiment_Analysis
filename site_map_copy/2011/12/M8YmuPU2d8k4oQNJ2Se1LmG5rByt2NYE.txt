Due to the condition of the building, it will be days before investigators can determine what happened, Conte said.Seven people lived in the house, Conte said. He had no immediate information on their identities or the conditions of the two who came out alive and were taken to the hospital.The home is in Stamford's Shippan Point neighborhood, just in from the Long Island Sound, fire officials said.
