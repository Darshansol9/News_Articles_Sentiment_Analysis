Story highlights One music fan in the UK wants him out of the Glastonbury FestivalHe's started a petition calling for organizers to replace him(CNN) Kanye West is known for rankling people on stage. West is booked to headline the festival, which runs between June 24-28. His performance is Saturday."Kanye West is an insult to music fans all over the world," Lonsdale says in the change.org petition.
