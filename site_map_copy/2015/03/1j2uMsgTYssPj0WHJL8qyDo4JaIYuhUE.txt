Story highlights The #TwizzlerChallenge is inspired by "Lady and the Tramp"Last year's Ice Bucket Challenge raised $115 million(CNN) Admit it, you've been secretly itching for a new cause since last year's Ice Bucket Challenge for ALS left your teeth chattering and raised $115 million.This one's goofier and tastier, and won't leave you needing a change of clothes. It's the #TwizzlerChallenge.So what's this challenge all about? Think of the spaghetti scene from "Lady and the Tramp," except substitute a Twizzler for the noodle.Chomp and chomp and chomp until your lips meet in the middle, then smooch. Simple enough.You're supposed to do a video, of course, and challenge others to do the same for a good cause.
