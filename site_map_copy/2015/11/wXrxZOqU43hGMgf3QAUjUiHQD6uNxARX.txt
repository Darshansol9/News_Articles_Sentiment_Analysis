Story highlights Jury finds Officer Lisa Mearkle not guilty in the shooting death of David KassickA video of the shooting has been made public"I never wanted to shoot anybody," said Mearkle, who wants to go back to her job in Hummelstown, Pennsylvania(CNN) A jury found a Hummelstown, Pennsylvania, police officer not guilty Thursday in the February shooting death of an unarmed man during a traffic stop.The shooting of David Kassick, 59, was caught on video and played at Officer Lisa Mearkle's trial."We're just glad the jury saw the video for what it was and didn't second guess," Mearkle's attorney Brian Perry said.
