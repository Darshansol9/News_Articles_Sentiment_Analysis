Story highlights Gorilla leaps toward exhibit window and hits it, sending family running.Zoo says patrons were never in danger.(CNN) A family trip to a Nebraska zoo turned terrifying for one family after the gorilla they were looking at leaped toward the exhibit window, cracking it.Kevin Cave caught the incident on video that he posted on his Reddit page. It has already been viewed more than 1 million times.Cave said when his family first arrived at Omaha's Henry Doorly Zoo gorilla exhibit, he noticed one of the gorillas had a cut below his eye that was "bleeding a little bit."He said he overheard a couple of zookeepers say the gorillas had been fighting with one another.Then the gorilla -- named Kijoto -- charged toward the window and smacked into it, he said.Read More
