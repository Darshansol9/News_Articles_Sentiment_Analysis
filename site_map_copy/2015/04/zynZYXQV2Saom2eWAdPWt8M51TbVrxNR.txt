During the family gathering, he dashed out into the street and was struck and killed by a GMC van, according to Milwaukee police.The distraught driver, Archie Brown Jr., 40, immediately stopped and got out to tend to the boy. But it was too late, police said. Damani was dead.Damani's alarmed family came running, including his older brother, 15-year-old Rasheed Chiles, police said.
