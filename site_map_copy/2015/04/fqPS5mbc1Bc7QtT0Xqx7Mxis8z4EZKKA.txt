The incident occurred at a popular surf spot near Big Beach at Makena State Park."The victim reportedly was snorkeling with two other friends and at some point separated from the others," the Fire Department said. "She was alone when she was found."Injuries on her upper torso suggested she was attacked by a shark, but there are no reported witnesses, it said.
