Story highlights $500,000 check left in kettle in Rosemount, MinnesotaIt's the largest single donation ever made to the Twin Cities Salvation ArmyCouple hopes their gift inspires generosity in others(CNN) The holiday spirit hit one Minnesota couple in a big way over the weekend.They dropped a $500,000 check into the Salvation Army kettle at a Cub Foods in Rosemount, Minnesota, on Saturday.Yes, you read that right. Half-a-million dollars.The couple wants to remain anonymous.
