(CNN) A six-alarm fire in the New York City borough of the Bronx burned through eight homes, injuring nine people and displacing 89 residents, a fire official said.Six of the injured were firefighters; three were citizens, said Lt. Craig Mosia with the New York City Fire Department. Seventy-four adults and 15 children were left without homes.The fire broke out Thursday just before midnight, Mosia said. It took fire crews three hours to get the blaze under control.
