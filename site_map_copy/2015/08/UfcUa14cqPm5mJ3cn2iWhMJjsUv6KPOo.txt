Story highlights The collapse took place during a Walker Brothers Circus performanceAbout 100 people were in the tent when severe weather hit(CNN) Two spectators were killed Monday when a circus tent collapsed in New Hampshire amid stormy weather, the state fire marshal said.The Walker Brothers Circus was performing a show in Lancaster when severe weather hit, Fire Marshal William Degnan said.About 100 people were inside the tent when it came down around 5:30 p.m., Degnan said.Officials didn't release the names of the deceased, only describing them as a "young man and a young girl."At least 27 people were hospitalized, representatives from two hospitals told CNN. It's unclear how many may have been taken to other hospitals.Read More
