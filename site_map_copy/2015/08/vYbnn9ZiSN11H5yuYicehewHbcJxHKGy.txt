Young II of East Canton.Young, 45, had been at the park with friends when he hopped on the Raptor around 5 p.m., Sandusky Assistant Police Chief Phil Frost told reporters.After losing some of his things, Young "jumped over a fence into a restricted area under the Raptor and was looking for his personal belongings," Frost said.
