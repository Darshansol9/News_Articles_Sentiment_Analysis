(CNN) Federal authorities have charged six people with aiding terrorists overseas.The Department of Justice accuses them of conspiring to provide materials and money to terrorists in Syria, Iraq and elsewhere. Two of them are accused of conspiring to kill and harm people overseas.The materials include U.S. military uniforms, boots, tactical gear and optical devices such as scopes.All six defendants immigrated to the United States from Bosnia, according to a Justice Department statement from Friday. Three of them are naturalized citizens.Five of the defendants have been arrested in the United States, while one is overseas, the Justice Department said.Read More
