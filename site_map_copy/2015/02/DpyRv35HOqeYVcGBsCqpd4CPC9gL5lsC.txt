A second request for reinstatement was heard Saturday by NASCAR's final appeals officer and also denied. NASCAR has not announced what Busch will have to do to be reinstated.Busch's attorney said after the first appeal was denied that his client will challenge the court's decision."We will continue to exhaust every procedural and legal remedy we have available to us until Kurt Busch is vindicated.
