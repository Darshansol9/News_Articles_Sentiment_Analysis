(CNN) One Marine died Sunday when an MV-22 Osprey experienced a "hard landing mishap" at Bellows Air Force Station in Hawaii.Twenty-one other Marines were also on board the military aircraft. They were taken to area hospitals for assessment and treatment, according to a statement from the Marine Corps.It said the Marines were conducting "routine sustainment training" at the time.The cause of the incident was not immediately clear. An investigation is under way.
