(CNN) Former fugitive Paul Jackson, who is accused of kidnapping women off the streets and raping them, is now back in the United States and behind bars.He was arrested in Mexico after more than 24 years on the run , authorities have said. Jackson was recently featured on CNN's "The Hunt With John Walsh.""The best news is he's now in Los Angeles. We got him back last night," Deputy Eric Wahlstrom of the U.S. Marshals Service Oregon Fugitive Task Force told CNN on Tuesday.He is expected to be in court Wednesday for an extradition hearing, according to the Hillsboro, Oregon, police.Lt.
