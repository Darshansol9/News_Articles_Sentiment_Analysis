Story highlights Two arrested in officer's deathAbilene police described the manner of death as "suspicious," but haven't released details(CNN) Texas authorities arrested two suspects Thursday evening in connection with the death of an off-duty Abilene police officer.Violetta Walter, 30, and Phillip Walter, 29, have been arrested on suspicion of first-degree felony murder. Each is being held on $500,000 bond."We were able to link the suspects to property taken from Don Allen's residence," a statement from the Abilene Police Department said. "We are confident that we have taken the correct people into custody. We do not believe there are any other suspects."Investigators have released few details in the case, but have described Allen's death as "clearly suspicious."Read More
