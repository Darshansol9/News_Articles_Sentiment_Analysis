(CNN) A 6-month-old baby left in an SUV at a Florida middle school was found dead Friday afternoon, authorities said.A teacher at Deltona Middle School who was caring for the child inadvertently left the boy in a car seat in the back row of an SUV, the Volusia County Sheriff's Office said.The sheriff's office received a 911 call at 3:54 p.m. and a school resource officer was sent to the vehicle, but it was too late.The sheriff's major case unit was assigned to the case.Temperatures in central Florida were in the mid-80's on Friday afternoon.
