Story highlights CNN Heroes set to announce Top 10 on Thursday during 8 a.m. ET hourVoting will begin to name CNN's Hero of the Year for 2015The Top 10 Hero with the most votes will receive an additional $100,000 for their cause(CNN) For months, CNN has been sharing amazing stories about everyday people doing remarkable things to make the world a better place.On Thursday during the 8 a.m. ET hour of CNN's "New Day," Anderson Cooper will reveal which of these CNN Heroes will be among this year's Top 10 CNN Heroes.Each will receive $10,000 and be honored at "CNN Heroes: An All-Star Tribute," a globally broadcast event that airs December 6.Immediately after Thursday's announcement, you can vote for the Top 10 Hero who inspires you most at CNNHeroes.com.
