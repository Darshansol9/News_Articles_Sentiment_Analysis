(CNN) Pope Francis has created a church tribunal to judge bishops who fail to protect children from sexually abusive priests, the Vatican announced Wednesday, a move long sought by abuse victims and their advocates.The new court will be part of the powerful Congregation for the Doctrine of the Faith, the Catholic Church's chief watchdog.
