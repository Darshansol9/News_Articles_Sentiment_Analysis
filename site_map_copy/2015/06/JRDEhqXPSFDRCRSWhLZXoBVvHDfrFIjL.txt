Story highlights Chicago wins the series over Tampa Bay 4-2This is the third NHL title for the Blackhawks since 2010(CNN) For the third time since 2010, the Chicago Blackhawks are the Stanley Cup champions.Chicago defeated Tampa Bay 2 to 0 on Monday night to claim the best-of-seven series in six games.The Blackhawks also won the NHL title in 2010 and 2013.Five of the six games were decided by a single goal.The sixth game was played in Chicago.
