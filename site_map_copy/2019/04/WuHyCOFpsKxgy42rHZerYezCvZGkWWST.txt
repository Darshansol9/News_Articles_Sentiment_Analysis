Durham, North Carolina (CNN) The cars near the site of a gas explosion that killed one person in Durham, North Carolina, on Wednesday look like they've been in accidents.Windows are blown out. Air bags are deployed. Some have dents and dings. The roads are awash in glass, so much so they glisten. The air reeks of dust and pollen.Some 60 emergency responders and cleanup people are on the scene of the blast, which happened hours earlier after someone smelled gas.Wednesday night, officials disclosed that the man who was killed was Kong Lee, the 61-year-old owner of the Kaffeinate coffee shop.Authorities said 17 people, including a firefighter, were hospitalized. Six people were critically wounded, said Lee VanVleet of Durham County Emergency Medical Services. Eight of the injured are employees of Duke University, school President Vincent E.
