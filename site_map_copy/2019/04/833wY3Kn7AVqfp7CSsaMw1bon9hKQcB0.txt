(CNN) Prosecutors have charged the suspect in the deaths of four people at a property management company in North Dakota with four counts of murder.Chad Isaak, 44, was arrested Thursday, days after the killings at the RJR Maintenance & Management company, according to Mandan Police Chief Jason Ziegler.He made his initial appearance in court Friday where a judge explained the charges to him and set his bail at $1 million.Isaak was not asked to enter a plea.
