(CNN) The zookeeper attacked by a tiger Saturday in Topeka, Kansas, is recovering."We're blessed to be able to say she's been moved out of intensive care late yesterday afternoon and still recovering at a hospital," Zoo Director Brendan Wiley told CNN's "New Day" on Monday."We're not sure how long she'll actually be there.
