(CNN) A Kentucky food truck is selling a T-shirt that says "I support LGBTQ -- liberty, guns, Bible, Trump, BBQ."Jamie Smith, owner of Belle's Smokin' BBQ, drew criticism after he posted a photo of the T-shirt on social media. LGBTQ commonly stands for lesbian, gay, bisexual, transgender and queer or questioning.Owner James SmithThe post quickly went viral, and generated outrage."Wearing that shirt is f------ offensive," Williamstown, Kentucky resident Ariel Lewis told CNN affiliate WXIX"But here's the bottom line, it's not just a simple acronym," Facebook user Chad Reynolds wrote.
