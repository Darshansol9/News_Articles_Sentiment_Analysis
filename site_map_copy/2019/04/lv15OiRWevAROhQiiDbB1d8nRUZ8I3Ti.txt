(CNN) Eric Holder, the suspect in the fatal shooting of Grammy-nominated rapper Nipsey Hussle, pleaded not guilty Thursday to one charge of murder and two counts of attempted murder, the Los Angeles County District Attorney's Office said.Holder, 29, was also charged at his arraignment with possession of a firearm by a felon.CNN reached out to an attorney for Holder but has not received an immediate response.He is being held on $5,000,000 bail, according to online jail records.
