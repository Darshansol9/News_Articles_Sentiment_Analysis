(CNN) An Illinois State Police trooper was killed early Saturday when he was struck by a wrong-way driver.He's the second Illinois trooper to be killed in three days, and the 16th to be struck by a vehicle in 2019.Trooper Gerald Ellis, 36, was on duty but headed home to his wife and two children on Interstate 94 in Green Oaks at about 3:25 a.m. when a wrong-way driver hit his squad car head-on, according to Illinois State Police.Ellis, an 11-year veteran of the Illinois State Police, was taken to a local hospital, where he died just after 4 a.m.The driver of the other vehicle also died, police said.Read More
