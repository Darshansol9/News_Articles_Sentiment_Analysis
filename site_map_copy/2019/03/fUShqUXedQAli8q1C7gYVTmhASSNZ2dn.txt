(CNN) Spring is officially here, and with it come the beautiful flowers and longer days to enjoy the sunshine. On the flip side, this means the start of pollen season and the yellow mess it leaves behind.Officials in LaGrange, Georgia, flew a helicopter over trees in the West Point Wildlife Management Area, stirring up yellow clouds of pollen."Pollen anyone?" they posted on Facebook.
