(CNN) Here's a look at what you need to know about the US Strategic Petroleum Reserve.Facts:The US Strategic Petroleum Reserve (SPR) is a stockpile of crude owned by the US government.It provides a back-up supply of crude oil if the commercial oil supply is disrupted.The crude oil is stored in underground salt caverns in a government complex along the Texas and Louisiana Gulf Coast. Salt formations are the cheapest, most environmentally safe way to store crude oil.
