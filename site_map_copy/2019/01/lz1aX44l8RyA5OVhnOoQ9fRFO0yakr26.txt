(CNN) An Alabama police sergeant was killed and another officer critically wounded in a shooting in Birmingham early Sunday, police said.The officers were investigating a call about vehicle burglaries when they encountered two suspects just before 2 a.m. local time, according to Birmingham Police Department Sergeant Johnny Williams."The officers approached the suspects to investigate when one suspect produced a weapon and began to fire at the officers," Williams said in a press release.The officers and one of the suspects were struck by gunfire and taken to UAB Hospital. A second suspect was taken into custody.Sergeant Wytasha Carter, 44, of Birmingham, later died of his injuries, police said. He is survived by his wife and two children.Read More
