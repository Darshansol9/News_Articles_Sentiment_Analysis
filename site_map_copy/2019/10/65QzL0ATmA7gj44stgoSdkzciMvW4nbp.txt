(CNN) A Florida surfer was riding the waves Sunday at New Smyrna Beach when he got one good bite of reality.Stephen Michelena, 32, was surfing in about 4 feet of water when he jumped off his board and landed on a shark, according to the Volusia County Beach Safety Ocean Rescue, on Florida's Atlantic coast.According to the rescue team, the shark bit the surfer's foot once and swam away.Michelena refused to be transported to the hospital and was treated on the beach for minor lacerations to his left foot."The shark was not seen well enough to estimate its size," the official Volusia County shark incident report said.Read More
