(CNN) You don't have to be a player for the New England Patriots to make an important interception.The Foxborough Police Department in Massachusetts arrested 33-year-old Zanini Cineus on Thursday and charged him with stealing memorabilia from an exhibit inside the Patriots' Hall of Fame.Among the stolen goods was a game-worn Tom Brady jersey, which Cineus was wearing underneath his jacket when police located him, according to police.But wearing the jersey apparently didn't give Cineus any of Brady's stellar movement in the pocket.
