(CNN) What would you do if you encountered a 98-pound Burmese python while out on a hike in Florida?Run? Freeze? The captors are members of the Florida Fish and Wildlife Conservation Commission 's Python Action Team.The female snake, which measures 18-feet, 4-inches, is the second largest wild python ever captured in the state, according to the commission.
