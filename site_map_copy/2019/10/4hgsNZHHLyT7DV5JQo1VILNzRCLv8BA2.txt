(CNN) The number of people who can now shout "Uno!" with their friends just got bigger -- and more inclusive.Mattel announced Tuesday that fans of the game can now buy UNO Braille . With more than 7 million blind and low-vision people in the US, Mattel worked with the National Federation of the Blind to make the game more inclusive."The fact that a blind person is now able to play a classic game of UNO straight out of the box with both blind and sighted friends or family members is a truly meaningful moment for our community," Mark Riccobono, President of the National Federation of the Blind, said in a news release.The deck will feature braille dots on the corner of each card, and all players call out the card as they play. The packaging of the deck also features braille on the front and back.The instructions and packaging of the new UNO Braille will feature braille dots.Instructions for the game can be downloaded in braille readable files.
