(CNN) A 9-year-old girl was mauled to death by dogs on Monday while playing in a Detroit neighborhood, police said.The girl, who has been identified as Emma Hernandez, was in an alley when the three pit bulls attacked her, according to a Detroit Police spokeswoman.Witnesses tried to save the girl and police said that someone shot one of the dogs.
