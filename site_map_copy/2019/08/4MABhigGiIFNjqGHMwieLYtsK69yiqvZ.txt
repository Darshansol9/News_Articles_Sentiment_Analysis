(CNN) As immigration authorities raided seven Mississippi food processing plants and arrested hundreds of undocumented workers on Wednesday, many children found themselves without their parents.In one heartbreaking video captured by CNN affiliate WJTV, an 11-year-old girl sobs and begs for Immigration and Customs Enforcement to let her parents go."Government, please put your heart -- let my parents be free with everybody else, please," she pleads.Christina Peralta, who said her daughter goes to school with the girl in the video, told CNN that after the girl finished school on Wednesday, some of her father's friends brought her to a community gym that had been housing children whose parents were detained."I need my dad ... mommy," the girl cries as she speaks with the station outside the gym.
