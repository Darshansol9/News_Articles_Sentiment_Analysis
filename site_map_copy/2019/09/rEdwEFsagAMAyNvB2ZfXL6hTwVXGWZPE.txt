(CNN) Newly released satellite images give a sense of just how devastating Hurricane Dorian was to parts of the Bahamas.Dorian was a Category 5 storm when it hit the Bahamas on Saturday and then stalled over the northern Bahamas for days before continuing on its path.The storm is blamed for at least 30 deaths and Bahamas officials say that hundreds, or even thousands are missing.Satellite images from Maxar Technologies show the damage to several parts of Marsh Harbour on Great Abaco Island, which suffered a direct hit from the storm.The images from before the hurricane were taken on October 25, 2018, and the photos after Dorian hit were taken on September 5.Read More
