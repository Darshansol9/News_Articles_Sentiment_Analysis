(CNN) A rescue dog in Bradenton, Florida, died while saving the family that had taken him in.After a fire began engulfing a home in the middle of the night, a Jack Russell terrier barked in alarm to help the family get out in time.However, the terrier, a rescue dog named Zippy, didn't survive the blaze, CNN affiliate Bay News 9 reports.Firefighters arrived at the house at 1:59 a.m., according to the City of Bradenton Fire Department."The family of four escaped unharmed, thanks in part to the family dog who alerted them to the fire," the department said in a Facebook post.
