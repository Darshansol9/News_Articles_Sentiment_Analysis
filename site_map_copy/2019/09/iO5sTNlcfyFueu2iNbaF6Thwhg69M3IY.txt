In photos: The September 11 attacksDaniel Hulshizer/AP Thick smoke rises over the New York City skyline after the World Trade Center towers were downed by terrorists on September 11, 2001.In an attack orchestrated by al Qaeda leader Osama bin Laden, 19 men hijacked four passenger planes on September 11, 2001.Two of the planes were intentionally crashed into the World Trade Center towers in New York.
