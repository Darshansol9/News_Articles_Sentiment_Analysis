(CNN) It's only the first day of school at Fond du Lac High School in Wisconsin, but already something's amiss: a snake is loose somewhere inside.Principal Matthew Steinbarth told CNN a three-foot long ball python named Kai escaped its cage in the science lab over the weekend.Steinbarth said class is still taking place in the lab since the snake is not a danger to the students. He said the snake is gentle, and he is not concerned with its disappearance.The snake is the pet of a biology teacher at the school, who brings it in on occasion to show the students.
