(CNN) Snickers just got a fall-themed update, just in time for pecan pie season.Mars, which makes the candy bar, debuted a Snickers Pecan Bar on Wednesday, conjuring up all sorts of chocolate pecan pie vibes. You feel them, too?This version was available only online, but by Wednesday night it had already sold out.Chances of buying any more sounded slim. A message on the website said customers could enter their email address to be notified "if we restock."The bars were being sold as a 15-pack for $30.
