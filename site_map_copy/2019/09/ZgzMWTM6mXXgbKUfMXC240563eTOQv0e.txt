(CNN) The driver of the tour bus that crashed in Utah, killing four tourists, was on his first trip with the company, according to the National Transportation Safety Board.The driver had recently been hired, NTSB investigator Pete Kotowski said during a press conference Sunday.The driver is back home in California and will be interviewed by investigators in the coming days, Kotowski said.A blood sample was taken from the driver by the Utah Highway Patrol, but the results have not been processed, he said.Kotowski said the California-based company that operated the tour bus has been in operation for "a short period of time," and is cooperating with the investigation and has provided any requested documentation.Read More
