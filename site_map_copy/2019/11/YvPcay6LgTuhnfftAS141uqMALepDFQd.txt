(CNN) While much of America was gathering around a dining table on Thanksgiving, this hospital stayed busy caring for not one, not two, not even three, but 12 sets of newborn twins.The cuteness overload occurred this week at Saint Luke's Hospital in Kansas City, Missouri. Staff believe it's the largest number of twins the hospital has ever cared for at once."We always see multiples in here, but not 12 sets at one time," nurse Danielle Gathers told CNN affiliate KMBCAnd, predictably, they're incredibly cute.Every set of twins was born this week -- all between five and 14 weeks early, a spokeswoman for the hospital told CNN.
