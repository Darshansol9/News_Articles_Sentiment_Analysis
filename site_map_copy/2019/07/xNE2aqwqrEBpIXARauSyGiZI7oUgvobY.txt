(CNN) This super-uncle ran straight into a burning building to save his 8-year-old niece when a fire broke out at their home.Derrick Byrd, 20, says he has fourth-, third- and second-degree burns but does not regret any of the steps he took to save his niece."I don't care if I was burning," Byrd said.
