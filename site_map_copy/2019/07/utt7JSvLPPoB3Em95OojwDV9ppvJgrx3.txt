(CNN) Cori "Coco" Gauff is just now beginning to process what it feels like to be at the next level of international tennis stardom."If somebody told me this three weeks ago, I probably wouldn't believe it," the 15-year-old said. "but I think just putting in the work definitely raised my confidence because I knew how hard I worked and I knew shots I could make and what was possible."Gauff lost to No. 7 Simona Halep in straight sets in the fourth round of Wimbledon on Monday.Halep, who was the world No. 1 in January, advances to the quarterfinals with a 6-3, 6-3 win. The 27-year-old Romanian has one major title: the French Open in 2018.After becoming the youngest player to reach Wimbledon through qualifying, Gauff entered the tournament ranked No. 313 in the world.
