(CNN) Employees at the State Line Lookout Cafe in Palisades Interstate Park in Alpine, New Jersey, got a surprise when they went to work on Monday.Hanging on the door was a bag of human bones.The employees contacted the Palisades Interstate Parkway Police Department to report what they suspected was a bag of human remains, which has since been confirmed.The Bergen County Prosecutor's office has launched an official investigation."The remains will be submitted to a lab for identification and will be compared to missing persons records," Bergen County Prosecutor Mark Musella said in a statement.Read More
