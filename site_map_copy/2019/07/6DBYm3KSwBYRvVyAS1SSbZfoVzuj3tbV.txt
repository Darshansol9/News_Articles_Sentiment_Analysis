(CNN) Here's some information about Washington, DC's subway system, generally called the "Metro." The Washington Metropolitan Area Transit Authority operates Metrorail, the second largest heavy rail system in the United States, and oversees the country's sixth largest bus system.Ridership on the rail system in 2018 totaled approximately 174 million trips; for the bus system, the total was 121 million trips and 2.3 million trips on MetroAccess, the paratransit service.The rail and bus system serves a population of four million passengers.The routes include trips from Washington to Maryland (Montgomery and Prince George's counties) and Virginia (Arlington, Fairfax, and Loudoun counties, and the towns of Alexandria, Fairfax and Falls Church).
