(CNN) Former NBA player Anthony Grundy has been arrested in connection with a fatal hit-and-run crash in Kentucky.The 40-year-old turned himself in to Louisville Metro police on Friday, CNN affiliate WAVE reported.Grundy told police he knew he hit something while driving on the highway the night of June 7 but didn't stop, WAVE reported. A woman who was trying to cross the road died after being struck, WAVE said. Her name was not immediately available.Grundy was charged not having a license or insurance, leaving the scene of an accident and failing to render aid, WAVE reported.CNN affiliate WTVD reported that Grundy appeared in court Saturday and pleaded not guilty.Read More
