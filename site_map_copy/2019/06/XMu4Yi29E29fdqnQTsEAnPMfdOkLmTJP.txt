(CNN) The American alligator populates nearly every swamp, lake and river (and occasional kitchen) in the southeastern US. But when Erin Weaver spotted one swimming near her Houston home, she suspected it was the one in danger."It looked like a steak knife that was sticking out of his head," she told CNN affiliate KTRKAlligators are frequent, almost welcome guests around the neighborhood.
