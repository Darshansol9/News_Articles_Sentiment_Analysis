(CNN) Another dead dolphin with a stomach full of plastic was discovered in Florida.The seven foot male dolphin washed ashore on Fort Meyers Beach in Big Carlos Pass, the Florida Fish and Wildlife Conservation Commission announced on Friday.The dolphin was found a week earlier, and during the necropsy a two-foot plastic shower hose was discovered inside the animal.This was found in the esophagus and forestomach of the animal."Your actions can make a difference - secure and properly dispose of trash, take part in coastal cleanups and share information on how to reduce marine debris with others," the FWC posted on Facebook.The FWC will have samples collected from the dolphin analyzed to determine the exact cause of death.
