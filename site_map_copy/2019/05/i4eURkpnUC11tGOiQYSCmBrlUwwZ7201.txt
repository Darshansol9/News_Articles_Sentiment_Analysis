(CNN) Ready to go to Mars? Well, you can get a boarding pass for a trek to the Red Planet, but only your name will make the trip.Let us explain.NASA's wants to launch its next rover to Mars next summer, with a planned landing in February 2021. The space agency is giving people the opportunity to have their names put on the rover (etched on microchips). The Mars 2020 rover will lay the groundwork for eventual human exploration of Mars.
