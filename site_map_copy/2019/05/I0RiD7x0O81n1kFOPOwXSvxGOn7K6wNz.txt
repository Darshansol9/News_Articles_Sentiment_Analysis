(CNN) Two people died in a helicopter crash Friday in a mountainous area of north-central Utah, authorities said.The helicopter went down north of Alpine, a community about 30 miles south of Salt Lake City, the Utah County Sheriff's Office said.Both people aboard were killed; their names were not immediately released.Authorities began searching Friday afternoon after a relative of someone aboard reported the helicopter overdue. It had taken off from eastern Utah's Uintah County, the sheriff's office said.Searchers found the wreckage shortly before 6:30 p.m. MT, the sheriff's office said.Read More
