While the risk for severe storms appears to be low, it extends over a large population.A severe thunderstorm watch is in effect until 10 p.m. Sunday for parts of New York, Pennsylvania and Vermont. A separate line of thunderstorms from the same system drenched northern Indiana and southern Michigan.
