(CNN) A 12-year-old is facing murder charges in the shooting death of a 10-year-old brother in Texas, authorities said.The shooting occurred Saturday in Conroe, the Montgomery County Sheriff's Office said in a statement.Deputies responding to the call found the boy with a single gunshot wound to the chest and transported him to a hospital, where he died.The motive of the shooting is unclear.
