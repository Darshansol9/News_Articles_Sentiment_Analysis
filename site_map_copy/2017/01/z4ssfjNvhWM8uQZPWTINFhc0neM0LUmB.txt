Story highlights Clayton is promoted to lieutenant at her funeral'She was surrounded by blue,' the police chief says(CNN) On the day she was laid to rest, Orlando police officer Debra Clayton received one final honor from her department. She was promoted to lieutenant.Clayton, 42, was killed Monday as she tried to confront a murder suspect at a Walmart.
