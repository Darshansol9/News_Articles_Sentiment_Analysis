(CNN) After one nine years in prison for a botched robbery, disgraced football star O.J. Simpson could be released by fall.A Nevada parole board granted him parole on Thursday.He has served nine years of a nine-to-33-year sentence for an armed robbery and kidnapping in Las Vegas in 2007.Simpson, 70, appeared alert, engaged, and quick to smile during his parole hearing, letting out a hearty laugh when Parole Board Chairman Connie Bisbee accidentally said he was aged 90. "I feel like it," he said , laughing.He said he was rehabilitated and ready to return to private life outside prison.
