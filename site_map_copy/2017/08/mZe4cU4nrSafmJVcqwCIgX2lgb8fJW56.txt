Bill Lowe of the Missouri State Highway Patrol said at a news conference.Authorities located McCarthy at 6:15 p.m. in a mostly rural area about 12 miles southeast of downtown Clinton -- where the deadly shooting took place on Sunday.He was not armed, Lowe said."I'm just glad he was apprehended," he said.Read More
