(CNN) As an off-duty police officer with the Waco Police Department prepared to head out and rescue people from Harvey's flooding, his daughter wrote him a heartwarming note.She seemed to know that what her father, Sgt. Scott Holt, was about to do was risky, but so important for the people left stranded and endangered by the storm."Dear Daddy, I hope you don't get hurt, but I hope you help other people that need it more than you do," she wrote. "I hope no one gets hurt more. I hope everyone gets away the hurricane. I love you!""I know this young lady personally," Sgt. Patrick Swanton wrote.
