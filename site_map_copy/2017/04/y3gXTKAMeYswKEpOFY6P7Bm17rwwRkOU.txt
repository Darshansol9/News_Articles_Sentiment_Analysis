Story highlights Golden Retriever may have ingested marijuana after walk in parkDog was acting lethargic, drunken(CNN) A Colorado dog owner is asking marijuana users to be more careful with their pot.Patty Moore called the veterinarian after her Golden Retriever, Chance, started acting strange, she told CNN affiliate KCNCMoore said the 10-year-old dog couldn't stand to eat.
