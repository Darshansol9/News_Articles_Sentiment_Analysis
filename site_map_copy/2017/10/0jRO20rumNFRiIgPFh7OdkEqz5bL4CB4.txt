Story highlights Lynne Anderson Powell died with one of her dogsHer husband and three other dogs survived(CNN) When towering flames swept over their neighborhood in Sonoma County, George and Lynne Anderson Powell started rounding up their four border collies."We had no warning and at 1 in the morning, we looked up and there was a wall of fire coming over the house," George Powell said.His wife grabbed their dog Gemma and drove down the road early Monday leading out of their rural neighborhood in her blue Prius.The other three dogs were terrified, so it took George Powell a few minutes to find them and get them in his car, he said.
