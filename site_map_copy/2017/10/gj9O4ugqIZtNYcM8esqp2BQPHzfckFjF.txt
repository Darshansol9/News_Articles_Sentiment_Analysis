Story highlights New Orleans officer on patrol was shot several times and died at hospital, police saySuspect surrendered and is under arrest(CNN) A New Orleans police officer was shot and killed in the line of duty in East New Orleans overnight, Superintendent of Police Michael Harrison said at a news conference early Friday morning.Officer Marcus McNeil, 29, was assigned to New Orleans' 7th District, and was on routine patrol along with three other officers shortly after midnight.When they exited their vehicle after observing what was described as suspicious behavior, the officers were immediately fired upon by a male suspect, Harrison said.One officer was struck and collapsed at the scene, while one or two officers returned fire, striking the suspect, according to Harrison.McNeil, who had suffered multiple gunshot wounds, was rushed to an area hospital where he passed away, Harrison told reporters outside the hospital.Read More
