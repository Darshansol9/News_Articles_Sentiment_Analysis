(CNN) William Cookson had begun the time-consuming process of repainting his home in Salina, Kansas, when he was deployed to Kuwait.So, a group of students from a nearby college jumped in."Not a lot of us college students got a lot of money, but we do have a lot of time," student Austin Workman told CNN affiliate KAKE . "So, this is the easiest way and the most thoughtful way of giving back."The students attend Salina Area Technical College and are part of the diesel program there. All 40 of them volunteered to help.By Wednesday, the students had power-washed the outside, cleaned out the gutters, scraped off the old paint and primed the wood on the turn-of-the-century house.Read More
