His vehicle veered onto the paved shoulder and hit a raised concrete curb and a guard rail, then overturned and came to rest on its roof, the CHP said.During the investigation, officers "discovered" Fisher had been drinking and he was arrested for DUI without incident, the CHP said.No other vehicles were involved.Read More
