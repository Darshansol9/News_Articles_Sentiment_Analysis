(CNN) Shirell Bates was shopping for school supplies for her kids when a police officer asked the North Carolina woman what was under her shirt.Bates, a mother of two, is pregnant with twins."Initially, I thought he was joking, so my response was, 'Twins,'" she told CNN affiliate WSOC . "I'm 34 weeks with twins. I'm having a boy and a girl."But when the officer didn't believe her, Bates realized something was off: She was being accused of shoplifting."At that point, to avoid him asking me again, I actually lifted my shirt just a little bit, just to expose my belly, so just he can see that I'm just a regular pregnant person buying school supplies," Bates told WSOC.Read More
