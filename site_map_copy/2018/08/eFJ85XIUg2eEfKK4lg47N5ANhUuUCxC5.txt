Hurricane Lane has weakened into a Category 1 storm, according to the Central Pacific Hurricane Center.Lane is expected to weaken to a tropical storm later tonight, but the threat of heavy rainfall and flooding continues.Lane was a Category 5 storm late Tuesday before weakening to a Category 4 Wednesday morning. It dropped to a Category 3 storm overnight Thursday. Lane was downgraded to Category 2 storm Friday morning.This was only the second time in recorded history that a Category 5 hurricane came within 350 miles of Hawaii — the first one being Hurricane John in 1994 — the National Weather Service said.
