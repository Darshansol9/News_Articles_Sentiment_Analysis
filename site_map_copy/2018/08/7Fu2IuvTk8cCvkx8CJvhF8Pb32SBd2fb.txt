(CNN) Jennifer Jones-Prothro and her husband James Prothro are expecting their ninth child in December, and she wanted this one's gender to be a surprise.The Vacaville, California couple learned the gender of all of their other children before they were born. Each time, Jones-Prothro's dad, Charlie, would insist that they not tell him."My dad always wanted to be surprised by it," she said. "There are not many surprises left in the world these days."Jones-Prothro's dad died last year after a long battle with cancer.
