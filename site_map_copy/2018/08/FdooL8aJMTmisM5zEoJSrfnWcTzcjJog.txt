(CNN) Firefighters have made major progress against a blaze threatening homes in Southern California -- with its containment nearly quadrupling within two days.The Holy Fire, one of more than a dozen burning in the state, was 41% contained by early Sunday, up from 10% on Friday.With the firefighters' progress, authorities lifted evacuation orders in several neighborhoods in Lake Elsinore, the city most threatened by the fire.About 11,120 people remain under mandatory evacuation, down from 21,000 on Friday.The blaze has scorched through canyons and mountains in Orange and Riverside counties since Monday, chewing through dry vegetation as it crept into residential areas.Read More
