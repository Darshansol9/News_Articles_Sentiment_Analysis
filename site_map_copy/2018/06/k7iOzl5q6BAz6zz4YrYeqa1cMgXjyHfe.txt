(CNN) The next edition of the Miss America pageant will scrap swimsuits and will be more inclusive to women of all sizes, the contest announced Tuesday.Gretchen Carlson, the chairwoman of the Miss America board of directors, announced on "Good Morning America" that the event will no longer feature a swimsuit portion.Miss America will be a competition, not a pageant, Carlson said on the show Tuesday."We will no longer judge our candidates on their outward physical appearance. That's huge," she said.Carlson also said the new Miss America competition will be more inclusive to women of "all shapes and sizes."Read More
