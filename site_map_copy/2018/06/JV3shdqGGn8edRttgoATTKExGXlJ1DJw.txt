(CNN) When a transgender woman got up to use the woman's bathroom at a restaurant, she said she was asked to show her ID.And when she refused, she said she got kicked out.Charlotte Clymer, who identifies as female, works for the Human Rights Campaign. She said she was at Cuba Libre Restaurant and Rum Bar in Washington on Friday with a group of friends for a bachelorette party.The restaurant is located in Northwest DC in the city's Penn Quarter neighborhood.Clymer wrote on Twitter that the group was having a good time. But things shifted when she and a friend went to use the bathroom.On her way there, an attendant at the restaurant asked Clymer for her ID, she wrote.
