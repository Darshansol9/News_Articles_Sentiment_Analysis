(CNN) Road rage was likely the cause of a shooting outside a business complex in Westminster, Colorado, on Thursday that left one child dead and three others injured, according to police.The Westminster Police Department responded around 3 p.m. local time to reports of a shooting, it said in a press release late Thursday. Officers discovered two adults and two children suffering from gunshot wounds in the parking lot of a business complex.Jeremy Webster.All of the victims were taken to a hospital, but a young boy died as a result of his injuries.After several hours, police said they had located and arrested the accused shooter, who they identified as 23-year-old Jeremy Webster.
