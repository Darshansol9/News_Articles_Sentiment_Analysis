(CNN) George Anthony, the father of Casey Anthony and an important witness in her murder trial, was seriously injured in a car crash on a Florida interstate Saturday afternoon.According to a Florida traffic accident report, Anthony was traveling west on Interstate 4 in Daytona Beach when his vehicle traveled off the right shoulder. When Anthony attempted to swerve left and back onto the roadway, he lost control of the vehicle and overturned several times.The report states that Anthony had a medical issue that may have caused him to drive off the road. The report didn't specify what the medical issue was.Anthony, 67, was transported to Halifax Medical Center with "incapacitating" injuries, the report said.An incapacitating injury is defined as a disabling injury such as broken bones or severed limbs that usually require hospitalization and transport to a medical facility.Read More
