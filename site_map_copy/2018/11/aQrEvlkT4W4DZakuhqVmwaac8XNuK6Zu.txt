(CNN) After a deadly attack last year on a mosque in Quebec, Christians and Jews formed human "rings of peace" around mosques across Canada to help Muslim worshippers feel safer on their way in.This past weekend, Muslims did the same for six synagogues in Toronto.Several hundred people, arriving in busloads from different mosques, stood shoulder to shoulder around the synagogues with one very clear message.A "Ring of Peace" outside the Temple Har Zion synagogue in Toronto."We're here for you," Fatima Sajan said.Sajan was one of the organizers with the National Council of Canadian Muslims for last weekend's "Rings of Peace."
