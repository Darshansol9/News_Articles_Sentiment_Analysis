(CNN) Bombs and chemicals sent through the mail are rare but terrifying. According to the US Postal Inspection Service, the postal service processes more than 170 billion pieces of mail every year and only a tiny fraction of those -- 16 over the last few years -- were investigated as mail bombs, says the US Postal Inspection Service.
