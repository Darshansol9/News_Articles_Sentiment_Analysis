(CNN) When firefighters rushed to the scene of a three-vehicle crash in Arizona, they were braced for the worst.A white pickup was sitting on top of a red sedan after what looked like a deadly collision with a black car.But remarkably, no one was injured in the freaky accident, according to the Yavapai County Sheriff's Office . And that is because everyone was wearing seat belts, authorities say.The woman who was driving the white pickup told local officials she was northbound on Williamson Valley Road when a black truck traveling in the same direction in the lane next to her made contact, causing her to lose control.She went into oncoming traffic on the southbound lanes and hit a black car, she told officials.
