(CNN) A white woman has been fired after video emerged of her confronting and blocking the entry of a black neighbor at a St. Louis apartment complex."Please move, miss," the black resident, D'Arreion Toles, is heard telling the woman on viral cell phone video he recorded late Friday as she blocked him from entering the downtown loft complex where they both live, CNN affiliate KMOV reported."I can," she said. "Do you live here?"The woman's employer said in a statement that she was fired after a review of the incident, the station reported."The Tribeca-STL family is a minority-owned company that consists of employees and residents from many racial backgrounds," the statement said.
