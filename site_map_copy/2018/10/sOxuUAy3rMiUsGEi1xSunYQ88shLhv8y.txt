(CNN) Chicago recorded another violent weekend, with five people shot fatally and another 36 injured, police say.Among the victims were a man and woman in their 20s who were riding in a vehicle together about 11 a.m. Sunday, and a 16-year-old boy shot about nine hours earlier, said Chicago Police Department spokesperson Laura Amezaga.All the shootings occurred between 6 p.m. Friday and 11:59 p.m. Sunday, she said.During the same time period the previous weekend, eight people were fatally shot and 22 more injured, Amezaga said.Chicago has struggled with high shooting and murder rates in recent years, but authorities say shootings and murders have been steadily decreasing.Read More
