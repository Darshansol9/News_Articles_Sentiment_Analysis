Johnny Bobbitt Jr., a homeless veteran with just $20 in his pocket, gave stranded motorist Kate McClure money for gas. McClure and her boyfriend then raised $400,000 to help him out. But the feel-good viral story devolved into a feud over how much money Bobbitt had yet to see, and a New Jersey judge has ordered the couple to turn over the money to him.
