(CNN) Two police officers were killed in a shootout early Saturday in Brookhaven, Mississippi, a state official said.The officers, identified as Cpl. Zach Moak, 31, and Patrolman James White, 35, were responding to a call about shots fired around 5 a.m., Mississippi Bureau of Investigation spokesman Warren Strain said.White was first at the scene, followed by Moak, an emotional Brookhaven Police Chief Kenneth Collins told reporters.From left: Patrolman James White, 35, and Cpl. Zach Moak, 31"We answer those calls many times and nothing ever happens but this morning something happened," Collins said.
