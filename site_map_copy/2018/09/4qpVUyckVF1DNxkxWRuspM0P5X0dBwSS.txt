(CNN) In 2011, Oregon romance novelist Nancy Crampton-Brophy published a WordPress article entitled, "How to Murder Your Husband.""As a romantic suspense writer, I spend a lot of time thinking about murder and, consequently, about police procedure," the article began.In June of this year, Crampton-Brophy's own husband of 27 years, 63-year-old Daniel Brophy, was found shot to death at the Oregon Culinary Institute where he worked.Three months later, Crampton-Brophy, 68, was arrested in connection with his death.According to a criminal complaint from the Circuit Court of the State of Oregon, Crampton-Brophy has been charged with murder and the unlawful use of a weapon.
