Peter Williston said.At least two gunshots were fired during dismissal time at the school, killing a 17-year-old girl and a injuring a 17-year-old boy, Birmingham police Chief Orlando Wilson said.Wilson said Wednesday that investigators considered the shooting accidental, but he did elaborate.Huffman was placed on a brief lockdown, and police were called to the scene.
