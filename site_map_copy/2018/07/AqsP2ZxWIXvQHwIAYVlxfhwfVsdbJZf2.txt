(CNN) A former assistant manager of a Florida sporting goods store says he was fired after tackling a man accused of trying to steal a gun.Tallahassee police say suspect Jason White went into an Academy Sports and Outdoors store on June 29 and asked to see a .40-caliber handgun. He is accused of grabbing the gun and then running for the exit.Dean Crouch had just finished helping another customer when he heard a commotion."All of the sudden, I heard 'stop that man' and I turned to look at the gun bar.
