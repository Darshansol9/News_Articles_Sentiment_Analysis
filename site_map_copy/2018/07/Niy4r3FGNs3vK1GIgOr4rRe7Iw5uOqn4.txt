(CNN) A 12-year-old entrepreneur says his lawn-mowing business is getting a lot of new clients after someone called the police on him for mowing the wrong yard.Reggie Fields runs Mr. Reggie's Lawn Cutting Service in the Cleveland, Ohio, suburb of Maple Heights. He has his cousins and his brothers and sisters working for him.The tiny landscaping crew was mowing a lawn last week when police showed up.Lucille Holt, the woman who hired Fields, told CNN affiliate WEWS that neighbors called the police because the children inadvertently strayed into a thin strip of their yard."They said the kids cut their grass," Holt told WEWS.Read More
