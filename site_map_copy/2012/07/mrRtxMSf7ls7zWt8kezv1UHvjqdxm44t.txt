by Jim Roope, CNN(CNN) Summer enrichment programs in low-income neighborhoods across the country are in trouble.Nine-year-old Nicole Levine at L.A.’s BEST Summer Program says that, without the program, she would probably just be at home watching TV. Something she'd rather not do.She could also wind up wandering the streets in her Los Angeles neighborhood as her single-parent mom works two jobs.Nicole’s grandfather Steven Levine says this neighborhood in the North Hills section of Los Angeles is a high-crime area with a significant amount of gang activity.
