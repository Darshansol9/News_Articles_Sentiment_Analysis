By the CNN Wire Staff(CNN) -- George Zimmerman was released from jail on Friday, one day after a Florida judge set bail at $1 million.Judge Kenneth R. Lester Jr. found that Zimmerman's deceit over cash holdings at his first bond hearing in April was not enough to hold him without bail. However, he also said he believed the suspect may have been planning to flee the country to avoid prosecution in the killing of Florida teen Trayvon Martin.Zimmerman had to post 10% of the $1 million -- or $100,000 -- to meet the requirement for release.Read More
