Story highlights Firefighters, two others suffer minor injuriesSix-alarm blaze took more than three hours to put outFire started on top floor apartment, spread to other unitsThirty people suffered minor injuries during an early morning fire at an apartment building in New York, a fire department official told CNN.A fire department spokesman said 28 firefighters and two other people were injured after a six-alarm blaze started just after midnight in a Bronx neighborhood. The fire ignited on the top floor of the six-story building, spread through some apartments, and eventually the roof collapsed.Firefighters fought the flames for about three and a half hours before putting it out.
