By Carl Azuz, CNN(CNN) Combining her efforts from the school year with some serious, sartorial creativity, Kara Koskowich wove together a garment that truly made the grade.The 17-year-old Canadian student graduated in a dress made from her homeworkThere was literally no chance of her running into anybody else with the exact same dress. Most graduates never want to look at homework again; Koskowich found a way to look good in it.She cut, sewed, glued, and eventually tailored a graduation gown out of the assignments that helped her graduate.
