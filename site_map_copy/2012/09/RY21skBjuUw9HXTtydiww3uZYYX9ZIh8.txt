From the CNN Wire StaffChicago (CNN) -- Hundreds of thousands of Chicago schoolchildren will return to class Wednesday after the teachers union voted to suspend its strike.About 800 union officers and delegates met for just over two hours before there was an overwhelming voice vote to suspend the walkout, according to delegates who attended the meeting.The contract agreement with the school system still needs to be ratified by the more than 29,000 teachers and support staff who are members of the union.
