Police did not indicate what the package was.Aerial footage from CNN affiliate WSB showed fire and other emergency vehicles responding to the incident.Offices near the package were evacuated, CDC spokesman Tom Skinner said before the all clear was given.The CDC is a federal agency that works to promote public health in the United States and around the world.
