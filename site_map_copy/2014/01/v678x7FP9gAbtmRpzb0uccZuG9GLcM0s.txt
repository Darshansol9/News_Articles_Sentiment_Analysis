While a gas leak could be one potential cause, it could be weeks until a cause is determined and may ultimately be undetermined," according to a statement released by the Minneapolis Fire Department.Most of the injuries suffered were burns or came from falls or jumps from windows, Robert Ball, a spokesman for Hennepin County Emergency Medical Services, said Wednesday.
