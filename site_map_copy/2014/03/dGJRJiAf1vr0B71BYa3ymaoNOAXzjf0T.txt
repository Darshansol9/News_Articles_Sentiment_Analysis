A single-engine plane crashed in Florida on Saturday, killing one person and injuring two others, the Pinellas County Sheriff's Office said.The plane was en route to the St. Pete-Clearwater International Airport from Chicago, the sheriff's office said. Deputies and the Federal Aviation Administration are investigating. It crashed in Clearwater.The three people on board the plane were from Round Lake, Illinois. Jeffrey Bronken, 53, was killed. The others on board were Katherine Bronken and Keyana Linbo, both age 15.
