I wasn't really afraid," a man walking his dog in downtown Los Angeles less than an hour after the quake told CNN affiliate KTLA. He didn't provide his name.CNN's Alan Duke said he was half awake in his Hollywood Hills home when he felt a jolt that lasted a few seconds."It just served as a rude awakening," Duke said.
