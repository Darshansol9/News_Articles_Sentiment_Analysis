Can you say extraordinary?I suspect that 6-year-old Lori Anne Madison can spell it - and it's one of the best words to describe her.The young girl from Prince William County, Virginia, has just become the youngest speller eligible to participate in the Scripps National Spelling Bee , according to the event's record books, which date to 1993. Mike Hickerson, the bee's communications manager, said there have been four spellers since 1993 who were 8 years old.Lori Anne, who is home-schooled, beat out 21 other kids in the county to win the bee, which enters her into the national bee.The word that thrust her into the spotlight?
