Rodriguez, CNN(CNN) -- For the first time, CNN exit polls show, Cuban-Americans in Florida voted for a Democratic candidate over a Republican, 49% to 47%.Cuban-Americans in Florida have reliably voted Republican and have been a factor in some presidential outcomes in the coveted swing state.In 2008, more Cuban-Americans voted for John McCain over Barack Obama, 53% to 47%.
