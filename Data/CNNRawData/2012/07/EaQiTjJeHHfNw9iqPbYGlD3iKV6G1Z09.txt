She also serves as a psychiatrist for between five and 10 patients, the resume states.University spokeswoman Jacque Montgomery said Friday the school had no immediate comment.Fenton has held many jobs over the years. She worked as a physician in private practice in Denver from 1994 to 2005, and was chief of physical medicine with the U.S. Air Force in San Antonio, Texas, in the early 1990s, according to the resume. Since 2008, she's won various grants and contracts to study schizophrenia.Fenton did her undergraduate work at the University of California, Davis and earned her medical degree from Chicago Medical School in 1986.Holmes is accused of opening fire inside a crowded midnight screening of the new Batman film last week, killing 12 people and wounding more than 50.
