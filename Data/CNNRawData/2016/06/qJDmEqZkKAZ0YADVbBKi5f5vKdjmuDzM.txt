(CNN) Newly released police call logs give a glimpse into the sheer chaos that unfolded inside Orlando's Pulse nightclub during the attack that killed 49 people and wounded 53 others.The calls between dispatchers and law enforcement give a raw, unfiltered look at what police were learning from 911 callers who were detailing the chilling observations as the attack unfolded. And it details attempts by dispatchers to make responding officers aware of what they might be up against when they arrived.
