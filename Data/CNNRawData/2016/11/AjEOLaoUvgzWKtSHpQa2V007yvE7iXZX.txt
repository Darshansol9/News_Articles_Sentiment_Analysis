Where will this year's travel trouble lie?AAA projects that 48.7 million Americans will travel more than 50 miles for Thanksgiving this week. That's a 1.9% increase from last year, and the most since 2007. Nearly 90% of travelers, 43.5 million, will be driving to their destinations.
