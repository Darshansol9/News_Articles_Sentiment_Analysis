Story highlights Unidentified police officer shot by suspect, but expected to surviveSuspect shot and killed early Saturday morning(CNN) A police officer in Anchorage, Alaska was ambushed and shot several times by a suspect Saturday, the city's police chief said.That suspect was shot and killed by another responding officer, said the Anchorage Police Chief Chris Tolley.The wounded police officer did not have life-threatening injuries and is expected to survive. Neither the suspect nor the police officer's names were released Saturday.The shooting occurred shortly after 4:30 a.m. when a police officer approached a pedestrian, whom he thought could be involved in a theft.That man held a gun and opened fire on the officer.
