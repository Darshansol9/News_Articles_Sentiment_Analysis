(CNN) A 9-year-old girl allegedly taken from school by an uncle with no custodial rights may be "in imminent danger," Tennessee Bureau of Investigation spokesman Josh DeVine said Tuesday.Authorities have received no credible sightings of Carlie Marie Trent, Gary Simpson or the vehicle Simpson was driving, DeVine said at a news conference.Simpson is an uncle by marriage to Carlie, whom he allegedly removed from school last Wednesday under false pretenses, a TBI news release said.Investigators know Simpson bought clothing and camping items, so it's possible they're in the woods or at a campground, DeVine said."There's every indication that he intended to go out of sight," DeVine said.
