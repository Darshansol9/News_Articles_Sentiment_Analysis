(CNN) A 13-year-old boy was hurt badly in a shark attack at a Florida beach on Sunday afternoon, police said.The incident took place in Neptune Beach about 2:55 p.m.The boy and his stepfather were swimming in chest-deep water when he was attacked, suffering an 8-inch bite behind his right knee and the top of his calf, Neptune Beach police spokesman Michael Key said.The injuries were serious but not life-threatening, Key said, and the boy was taken to UF Health Jacksonville."He was pulled out by bystanders," Key said.
