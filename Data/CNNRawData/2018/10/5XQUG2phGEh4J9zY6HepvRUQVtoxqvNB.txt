(CNN) A California school district has reassigned a teacher who said Trump aide Stephen Miller would eat glue off his arm when he was in her third grade classroom.Nikki Fiske is a teacher in the Santa Monica-Malibu Unified School District.In a piece in The Hollywood Reporter , she said that when Miller was 8, he would pour glue on his arm, wait for it to dry, peel it off and eat it."I remember being concerned about him -- not academically," Fiske said in the piece, which appeared Wednesday. "He had such strange personal habits. He was a loner and isolated and off by himself all the time."A district spokesman told CNN it is reviewing the incident but that Fiske has been reassigned.Read More
