(CNN) Earlier this month, US Postal Service letter carrier Kierra Coles left her Chicago apartment apparently dressed for work.A neighbor's surveillance video captured her walking past her vehicle in her work uniform, CNN affiliate WLS reportedColes was last seen on October 2, Chicago police said. She is three months pregnant, WLS reported.Chicago police said in a statement they "suspect possible foul play" in the 27-year-old's disappearance because she has been missing for more than two weeks. The reward for information leading to Coles' whereabouts is now more than $28,000."I miss the smile on her face. If anybody knows anything, please, call in.
