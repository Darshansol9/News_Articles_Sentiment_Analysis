(CNN) For almost 30 years, John Chhan and his wife, Stella, ran the Donut City shop in Seal Beach, California, a constant, reassuring part of countless customers' lives. But now the hard-working couple is facing a medical crisis, and their patrons are returning the sweetness.Since Stella suffered a brain aneurysm last month, John has longed to spend as much time as he can with her."She's in a nursing home," Chhan said. " My sister-in-law is here helping me."With a business to run and bills to pay, the doughnuts still have to roll.
