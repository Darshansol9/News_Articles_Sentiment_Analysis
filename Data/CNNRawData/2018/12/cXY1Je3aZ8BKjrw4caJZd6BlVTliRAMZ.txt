(CNN) Chris Watts, the man who killed his pregnant wife and two children, has been transferred from a Colorado prison to Wisconsin.Watts pleaded guilty last month in a Colorado court to first-degree murder and other charges in the death of his wife and young daughters. He was sentenced to five life sentences with no possibility of parole.Authorities said this week he had been transferred outside of Colorado for his own safety. He is now in the Wisconsin Department of Corrections system, according to online records.His wife, Shanann Watts, and their daughters, Bella, 4, and Celeste, 3, were reported missing August 13.
