(CNN) These life-size "little green army men" march, fight, crawl and pose -- and now women can, too.For the first time, the Green Army Patrol will include both male and female soldiers.The co-ed troops, based on the popular, plastic toys from the "Toy Story" movie franchise, will debut Saturday at Toy Story Land, Disney's latest theme park addition at its Hollywood Studios in Orlando.Tom Vazzana, show director at Walt Disney World Resort, said the move to include women in the Green Army Patrol was not "a ginormous political statement" but that "it just made sense.""We didn't create a wheel; we just made the wheel better," Vazzana said.Read More
