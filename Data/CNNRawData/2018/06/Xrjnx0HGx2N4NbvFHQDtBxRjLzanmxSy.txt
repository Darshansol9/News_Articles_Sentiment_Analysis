(CNN) Dozens gathered Wednesday night at LaGuardia Airport to support separated immigrant children.Activists urged supporters via social media to come witness the arrival of children, whom they believed had been separated from their families, who were landing in New York. It was not immediately clear whether those children had been separated from their families.Crowds gathered at the airport with signs in Spanish reading: "We love you" and "We are with you." CNN crews saw supporters lining the terminal, holding signs and showing messages of support.
