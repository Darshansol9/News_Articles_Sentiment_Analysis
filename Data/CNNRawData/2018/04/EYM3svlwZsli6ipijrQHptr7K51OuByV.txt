(CNN) Residents of Antioch, Tennessee, awoke Sunday to find their neighborhood was back in the headlines, after a barely-clothed gunman opened fire at a Waffle House, killing four people.Sunday's shooting happened almost 7 months after a gunman entered an Antioch church with two pistols . On September 24, 2017, Emanuel Samson began firing indiscriminately inside the Burnette Chapel Church of Christ, killing one churchgoer and wounding 7 others.The sites of both shootings are just 2 miles down the road from each other.Antioch is a neighborhood in Davidson County governed by the city of Nashville."Tragedies like today shouldn't happen," said Nashville Mayor David Briley in a press conference Sunday afternoon.
