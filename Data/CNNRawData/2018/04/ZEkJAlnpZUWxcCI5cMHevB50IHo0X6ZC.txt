(CNN) Ask anyone around Houston to name their favorite pizza joint, and the answer's likely to be unanimous: Fuzzy's in west Houston.Ask the guy known as Fuzzy about his favorite customer, and the answer will surprise you: Barbara Bush.Because if it weren't for her and her husband George H.W. Bush, Fawaz "Fuzzy" Hajjar says he wouldn't have the success he has today.Hajjar immigrated from Syria with just $50 in his pocket and after years of scrimping and saving, opened a pizza shop. And then, Barbara Bush - during an appearance on David Letterman -- mentioned it as her favorite place to eat in Houston.And the rest is history: a beautiful history of an American dream and a first lady who helped nurture it.Read More
