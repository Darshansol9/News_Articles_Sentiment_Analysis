(CNN) A man has been arrested in connection with a shooting that left two Dallas police officers critically wounded, Chief Reneé Hall said.Police had launched a manhunt for Armando Luis Juárez, 29, earlier on Tuesday.Juárez was arrested Tuesday night following a brief chase. He is facing multiple counts of aggravated assault to a police officer, Hall told reporters.The incident began Tuesday afternoon when an off-duty officer at the Home Depot called to request backup officers for an arrest.The two officers who responded to the call were wounded, Hall said.Read More
