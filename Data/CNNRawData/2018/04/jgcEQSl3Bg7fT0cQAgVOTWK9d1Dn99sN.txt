However, in Puerto Rico, the wage gap is every so slightly flipped.According to the US Census Bureau , the median salary for women in Puerto Rico was $24,486 in 2016, the most recent year for which such data is available. The median salary for men was $23,711. This amounts to about a 3% difference in pay.The slight gap isn't a new thing, either.
