(CNN) Charities are providing immediate assistance for those affected by the volcanic activity on Hawaii, and their efforts can be supported through the Public Good campaign button below.Since the Kilauea volcano erupted on Hawaii's Big Island on May 3rd, residents have faced an ongoing disaster.
