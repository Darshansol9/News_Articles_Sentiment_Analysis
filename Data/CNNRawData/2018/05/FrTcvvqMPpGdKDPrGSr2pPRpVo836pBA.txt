Vance Jr. said in a statement Monday that Ortega violated the trust of a family in the "most horrific way imaginable" and that "Leo and Lulu will never live the lives that laid ahead of them."Ortega said Monday she was "very sorry for everything that happened.""My life is in the hands of God," she said.Read More
