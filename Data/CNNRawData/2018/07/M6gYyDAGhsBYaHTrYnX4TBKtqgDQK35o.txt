(CNN) One of the suspects sought in a nationwide manhunt connected to the alleged rapes of two teenage Ohio girls was arrested Thursday in Texas, according to US Marshals.David Ramos Contreras, 27, of Mexico, was arrested in Lubbock in northwest Texas."The diligent and dedicated work of our fugitive task force, along with the US Marshals in Lubbock and our local partners enabled this arrest to happen quickly and safely," US Marshal for the Northern District of Ohio Pete Elliott said.Authorities said tips and other leads led to the arrest of Contreras, who was captured while hiding under a bed.Two suspects are still on the loose.
