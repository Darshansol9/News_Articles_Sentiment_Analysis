(CNN) Michigan school districts can ban firearms on campus, the state's Supreme Court ruled Friday.State law allows people with concealed firearms permits to bring guns onto school property, but at least two districts enacted policies banning weapons.Separate lawsuits were filed by firearm advocacy groups against the Ann Arbor and Clio school districts."We feel like it's a great day for Michigan children," Jeanice Swift, superintendent of the Ann Arbor Public Schools, said. Swift said the policy, which is tied to a city ordinance about school disruptions, was enacted after someone legally carried a gun into a choir concert in March 2015.Read More
