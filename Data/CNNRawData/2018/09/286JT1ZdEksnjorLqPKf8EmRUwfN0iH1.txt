(CNN) Once again, Pennsylvania authorities are trying to figure out what made correctional officers sick -- this time at a Pittsburgh jail.Eleven Allegheny County Jail staff fell ill from an unknown substance or odor Sunday night, jail warden Orlando Harper said in a statement.The nine corrections officers and two medical personnel were treated and released from a local hospital. But the county jail remains on lockdown Monday"Staff are currently interviewing inmates and gathering intel into what may be causing this illness," Harper said.
