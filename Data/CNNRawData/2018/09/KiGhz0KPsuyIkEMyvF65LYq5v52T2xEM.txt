(CNN) Two young children died in separate incidents after being left alone in hot cars Friday in Florida, police said.A 1-year-old girl was found dead in a car at a Wawa gas station in Sanford, north of Orlando, said Seminole County Sheriff's Office spokesman Bob Kealing.The fire department responded to a report of a child possibly locked in a car at a gas pump about 4:50 p.m., Kealing said.Deputies were questioning the child's mother.
