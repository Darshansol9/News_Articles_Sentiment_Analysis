(CNN) A Virginia man took "you kids get off of my lawn" to new levels by putting up an electric fence near a spot where students wait for the school bus.Bryan Tucker said he erected the fence Tuesday because he had trouble last year with kids trespassing and littering on his property."They don't respect other peoples' land," Tucker told CNN affiliate WTVR .
