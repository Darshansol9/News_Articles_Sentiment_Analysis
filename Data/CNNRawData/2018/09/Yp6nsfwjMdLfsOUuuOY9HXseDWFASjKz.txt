(CNN) For weeks and weeks, it burned -- the largest wildfire in California history.It devoured close to 459,000 acres and killed one firefighter who was among hundreds who came in from across the country to help put it out.This week the Mendocino complex fire, which started in late July, is finally fully contained."The Ranch Fire is now 100 percent contained and the River Fire is 100 percent contained," began a statement from the US Forest Service released Wednesday.The Mendocino fire consisted of twin flames -- the River Fire and the Ranch Fire.
