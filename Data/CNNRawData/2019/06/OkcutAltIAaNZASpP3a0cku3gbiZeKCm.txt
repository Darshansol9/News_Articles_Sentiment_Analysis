(CNN) DJ Pryor had just finished watching the season finale of "Empire," unsure of how he felt about it.He began talking about it, to no one in particular. That's when his 19-month-old son, Kingston, looked up, climbed onto the couch and began "talking" with his father about the finale.The only thing is, Kingston can't talk. But no one would know that from watching the video of Pryor and his son's conversation, which had garnered more than 47 million views on Facebook by Friday night."I was laughing because of the way he was doing all the hand gestures," Pryor, a comedian , told CNN on Friday.
