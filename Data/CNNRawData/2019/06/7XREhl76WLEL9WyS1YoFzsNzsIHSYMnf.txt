(CNN) Whenever New York Gov. Andrew Cuomo hears of an aircraft hitting a building, the September 11 terror attacks immediately come to mind.Monday was no different, when a helicopter crash-landed on the roof of 787 Seventh Ave. in midtown Manhattan."If you're a New Yorker, you have a level of PTSD from 9/11. And I remember that morning all too well," he said."So as soon as you hear an aircraft hit a building, my mind goes where every New Yorker's mind goes."New York Mayor Bill de Blasio said nothing suggests the crash was a terror attack, although the cause is still under investigation.
