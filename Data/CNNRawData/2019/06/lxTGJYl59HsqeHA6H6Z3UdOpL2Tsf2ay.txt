(CNN) Someone in California is super rich. About $530 million wealthier -- or a cash option payout of $345.2 million.A single winning ticket sold in the state matched all six numbers of the Mega Millions jackpot Friday night. The numbers were 17, 19, 27, 40 and 68 -- with a gold Mega Ball 2, contest officials announced early Saturday.It is the seventh-largest jackpot in the history of Mega Millions drawings and the largest since last October, the organization said. That jackpot came in at $1.5 billion.The jackpot has been accruing since it was last won in Missouri on March 12.
