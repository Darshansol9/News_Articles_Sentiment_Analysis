(CNN) A South Carolina father killed all five of his children in 2014. Still, his ex-wife argued for mercy.But the jury wasn't having it -- on Thursday, they sentenced him to death.Timothy Jones Jr. had full custody of children -- Mera, 8, Elias, 7, Nahtahn, 6, Gabriel, 2, and Abigail 1 -- after his divorce from Amber Kyzer in 2012. Two years later, he killed them, dumped their bodies in trash bags, and left the bags off of a dirt road in Alabama.When the trial began in May, Jones pleaded not guilty by reason of insanity. He was found guilty earlier this month.But Kyzer appealed to the jury to say she didn't want her husband's family to feel the pain of losing a son like she did.Read More
