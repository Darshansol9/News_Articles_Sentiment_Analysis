(CNN) A gunman killed two people Tuesday evening at a Ford dealership south of San Jose, California, police said.When officers arrived on the scene in Morgan Hill around 6 p.m., they found a man on the ground outside with what appeared to be a self-inflicted gunshot wound and a handgun by his side, police said.Two employees were found inside the dealership suffering from gunshot wounds.
