(CNN) A Georgia inmate has been executed for the murders of two women in 1994, the state's Department of Corrections said.The execution of Scotty Garnell Morrow, 52, was carried out at 9:38 p.m. Thursday at the Georgia Diagnostic and Classification Prison in Jackson."Morrow accepted a final prayer and recorded a final statement," the department said in a statement.Morrow is the 73rd person to be executed in Georgia since the US Supreme Court reinstated the death penalty in 1976.
