(CNN) A Miller Park Zoo flamingo was euthanized Monday after an elementary school student threw a rock inside the animal's exhibit.A representative from the Bloomington, Illinois, zoo told CNN affiliate WMBD the rock broke the flamingo's leg and caused injuries that led to it being put down.The zoo said it's working with the student's family to make sure this is a learning experience, WMBD reported.The greater flamingo exhibit at the zoo opened in June 2016 , according to the city of Bloomington.According to the University of Michigan , greater flamingos are found in the Middle East in countries like Iran, Turkey and Afghanistan, as well as in areas across west Africa, South America and throughout Europe.
