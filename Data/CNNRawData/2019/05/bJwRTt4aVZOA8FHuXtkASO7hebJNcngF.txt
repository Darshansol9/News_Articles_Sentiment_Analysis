(CNN) It might be May but there is a winter storm warning for part of California, where up to 3 feet of snow might fall.In what is more like a January weather pattern, a strong storm will push through California over the next couple of days.Snow will begin in the California Sierra on Wednesday night and up to 2 to 3 feet of snow is possible through Friday morning at the highest summits.Yosemite National Park announced that Glacier Point Road was closing because of the storm.Rain will spread over much of the rest of the state.
