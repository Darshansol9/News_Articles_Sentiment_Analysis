Kirstin was at the Gilroy Garlic Festival during the deadly shooting on Sunday.At least three people are dead and 12 injured after a shooting Sunday night at the Gilroy Garlic Festival in Northern California.The suspect -- identified as Santino William Legan, 19 -- died from a self-inflicted gunshot wound to the head, Gilroy Police Chief Scot Smithee said.The shooting happened at the end of the final day of the festival, about 30 miles southeast of San Jose. Witnesses described pandemonium, with people running, hiding behind tents, crawling under tables or climbing fences to escape the gunfire.
