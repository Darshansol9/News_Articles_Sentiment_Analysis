(CNN) Police are investigating after a 9/11 memorial dedicated to first responders was found vandalized early this week in Geneva, New York.Police received a complaint of spray-painted "scribbles" on the memorial, though there didn't seem to be anything specific written, assistant city manager Adam Blowers told CNN.The memorial, located on Long Pier on Seneca Lake, was installed in September 2016, according to CNN affiliate WHAMPhillip Herrick, a Geneva resident, told CNN affiliate WROC he doesn't think the damage is significant."I think it's kids being kids, doing stupid stuff," he said.
