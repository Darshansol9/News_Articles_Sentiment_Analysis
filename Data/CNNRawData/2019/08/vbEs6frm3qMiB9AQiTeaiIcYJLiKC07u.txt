(CNN) A 70-year-old woman fell to her death in the Grand Canyon National Park, the park said in a news release.Park rangers responded to a call of a person needing help at rocky point west of Pipe Creek Vista on Tuesday, the park said. But before the rescue operation began, the woman fell.Her body was later recovered by the park's helicopter and rescue team of about 15 people, the release said.The woman's death follows a string of others since late March.Earlier this month, a 67-year-old man died after falling over the edge of the Grand Canyon, near the Yavapai Geology Museum.
