(CNN) A new Monmouth University poll surveyed the top eight selling candies for Halloween and Reese's Peanut Butter Cups came out on top.The poll surveyed a random sample of 1,161 adults age 18 and older about their Halloween favorites.Thirty-six percent love the peanut butter and chocolate combo, while just 18% favor second place Snickers. M&M's came in at number three with 11%.The other five candies named in the poll received 6% or less of the votes.
