(CNN) Six people, ranging from 14 to 27 years old, are being treated in a Philadelphia hospital after being shot, police said.A 911 call came in Sunday evening reporting "gunshots and several victims," Philadelphia police said."At 8th Street and Clearfield Street on the highway at approximately 5:24 p.m., E-911 received a call for gunshots and several victims shot on the highway," Philadelphia police said in an emailed statement, adding that there are "currently 6 victims at Temple University Hospital."Four of the victims are in stable condition, Philadelphia police said.A 21-year-old man was shot once in the left shoulder and is in stable condition.
