(CNN) The Latin American Music Awards (AMAs) feel and sound like the best house party you've ever attended.The one-night celebration of the best Latin music, as voted by fans, features artists from every corner of the industry, from regional Mexico to tropical pop and trap. And the 2019 nominees are stacked with talent.Many of this year's nominees may be strangers to Latin AMAs novices -- but they shouldn't stay that way.Several are already chart-topping stars in their respective genres and have been for years.
