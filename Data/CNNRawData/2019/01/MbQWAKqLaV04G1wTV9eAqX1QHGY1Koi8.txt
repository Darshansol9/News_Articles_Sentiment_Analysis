(CNN) A Texas mom says her 6-year-old son met "the real Santa" last week at a Fort Worth sporting goods store.Matthew Foster is blind, has autism and hates posing for pictures, but he's really interested in Santa, his mom, Misty Wolf, told CNN.Wolf said she wanted to get to the Cabela's store early to avoid the crowds and minimize the stares if Matthew or his 20-month-old sister Lilly had a meltdown.The plan worked and they were able to walk right up to see Santa.Wolf said Matthew does talk, but he doesn't interact with people unless he feels comfortable with them.Read More
