(CNN) An airplane crashed Friday afternoon at an air show in Florida, killing the pilot, Martin County Sheriff William Snyder said.The male pilot was the only person aboard and no one on the ground was injured, the sheriff said.Snyder's office tweeted a photo of the turboprop plane and emergency responders.
