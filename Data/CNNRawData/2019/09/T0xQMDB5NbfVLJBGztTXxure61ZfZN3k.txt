(CNN) A Florida man with Down syndrome died in a hot van while his caretaker took a nap, the Pinellas County Sheriff's Office says.Joshua Russell, 26, of St. Petersburg was charged last week with one count of aggravated manslaughter of a disabled adult.Authorities said 35-year-old John LaPointe of Seminole died while restrained in the back of the van on a hot day in the spring."LaPointe had Down syndrome, was non-verbal, had the cognitive ability of a 1-year-old, and was unable to care for himself," the sheriff's office said in a statement."This poor guy baked," Sheriff Bob Gualtieri said Friday at a press conference.
