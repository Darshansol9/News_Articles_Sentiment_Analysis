(CNN) When Anthony Rogers's beloved dog Bobo went missing, he was despondent. The two are homeless, he told CNN, but they've found family in each other and their midtown Memphis community.So when they reunited, naturally, it was nothing short of enchanting.In a video from Memphis Animal Services, Bobo bounds into Rogers' arms, his tail wagging furiously. He can hardly contain his excitement when he's reunited with his owner, skittering around him with heaping licks of love.A missing friendRogers woke up early in the morning August 31 to find Bobo had disappeared.
