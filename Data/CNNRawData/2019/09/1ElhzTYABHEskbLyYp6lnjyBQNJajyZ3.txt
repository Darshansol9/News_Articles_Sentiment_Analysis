(CNN) Antonio Brown took the field Sunday in his first game as a New England Patriot, five days after he was sued on allegations of rape and sexual assault.His appearance against the Miami Dolphins ended days of speculation over whether the NFL would keep him sidelined while it investigated the claims.The Patriots wasted no time playing their newest wide receiver.Brown caught all three passes thrown by quarterback Tom Brady on the team's opening drive. Later he caught his first touchdown of the season in the second quarter.But off the field, Brown's accuser and the NFL are planning to discuss her allegations this week.
