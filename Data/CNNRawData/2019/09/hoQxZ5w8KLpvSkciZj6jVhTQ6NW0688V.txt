(CNN) Bob and Shirley Hansen were on a fishing trip when they woke up around 3 a.m. Monday to the sound of someone beating on the side of their boat.Bob looked over the side and saw five people in their underwear, he told CNN affiliate KEYT-TV . Then he saw another boat in the distance, fully engulfed in flames. Hansen said he immediately called the Coast Guard."Flames probably 30 feet high.
