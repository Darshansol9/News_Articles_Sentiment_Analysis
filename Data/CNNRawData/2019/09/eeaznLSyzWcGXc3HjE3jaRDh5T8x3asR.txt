(CNN) Maryland police shot and killed a man who stabbed five people Saturday, authorities said.Reports came in around 1:45 p.m. ET of a man threatening people and demanding money at a liquor store at the Hunt Valley Towne Centre in Cockeysville, near Towson, Baltimore County police spokesperson Jen Peach said during a press conference.The man stabbed four people in the liquor store, then fled and stabbed another person in front of a Noodles and Company restaurant, Peach said.All five victims had non-life threatening injuries, Peach said.Witnesses told CNN affiliate WJZ-TV that liquor store employees asked the man to leave the store twice.
