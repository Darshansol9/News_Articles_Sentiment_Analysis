(CNN) Three people were shot and a pedestrian was hit by a vehicle during a chaotic night outside the main gates of the Minnesota State Fair.Monday was the final day for this year's fair, which is located between the cities of Minneapolis and St. Paul.First, a 19-year-old woman who had been struck by a passing vehicle was found by St. Paul police late Monday.Witnesses said there had been a fight in the area just before the woman somehow ended up in the road and hit, according to a police statement. The driver of the vehicle initially stopped but left the scene after bystanders began kicking and hitting his vehicle. He drove a short distance away, pulled over and called 911.He is cooperating with investigators, who report no signs of impairment.
