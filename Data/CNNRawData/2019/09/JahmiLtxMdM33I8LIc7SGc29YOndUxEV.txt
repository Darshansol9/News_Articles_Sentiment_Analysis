(CNN) The suspect in a mass stabbing Wednesday at a Florida workplace is facing charges of attempted homicide and aggravated assault with a deadly weapon without the intent to kill, according to court records.Antwann D. Brown, 41, is accused of stabbing five co-workers with a folding knife at Dyke Industries, Tallahassee police said. Brown sought out his victims, police said.He faces five counts of attempted homicide and one count of aggravated assault with a deadly weapon without the intent to kill.Authorities respond to the scene at Dyke Industries, a door manufacturer.All five stabbing victims were taken to Tallahassee Memorial HealthCare.
