(CNN) Jamario Howard was sitting with his friends Jamychol and Tae at a barbecue joint in Oxford, Alabama, waiting for food, when he noticed an elderly woman sitting alone."My first thought was 'dang I'd hate to have to eat alone,'" he told CNN. So he went over and asked her if he could sit with her.The woman said yes, and after they talked for a while Howard found out she had lost her husband and the day after would have been their 60th anniversary."I instantly gave my condolences and asked her to come eat with us," Howard said. "She was excited to do so."Howard shared a picture of the heartwarming moment, which happened April 18, on Facebook. It went viral with more than 44,000 shares.Read More
