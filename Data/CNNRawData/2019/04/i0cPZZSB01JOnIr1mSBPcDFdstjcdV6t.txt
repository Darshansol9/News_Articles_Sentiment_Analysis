(CNN) A suspect involved in the shooting death of a sheriff's deputy was fatally shot in an exchange of gunfire with law enforcement Sunday, the Cowlitz County Sheriff's Office in Washington said.Sheriff's Deputy Justin DeRosier, 29, was shot and killed on Saturday night.Sunday, officers responded to a report of a suspicious person near Spencer Creek Road in Kalama, Washington, and shots were fired when they engaged with the suspect at the scene, the sheriff's office said.The suspect was killed in the gunfire exchange. No officers were injured, the sheriff's office said.Police have not released the name of the suspect.Read More
