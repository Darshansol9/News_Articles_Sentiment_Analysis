(CNN) Someone is attempting to sell the video that allegedly shows New England Patriots owner Robert Kraft during a prostitution sting at a Florida day spa, a court motion says.The motion filed Thursday by attorneys representing the two women accused of running the Jupiter spa that Kraft is accused of patronizing, accuse law enforcement of violating a temporary protective order by leaking the video.Attorneys cited a report published Thursday on a celebrity news website that claimed it was "recently contacted by a party who claimed to have obtained portions" of the video.The website's representatives did not specify who contacted them."Considering the fact that only the Palm Beach County State Attorney's Office and the Jupiter Police Department has possession, custody, and control of the video surveillance, it is a logical conclusion that someone must have leaked the video surveillance, in direct violation of this Court's Order," the attorneys wrote.Read More
