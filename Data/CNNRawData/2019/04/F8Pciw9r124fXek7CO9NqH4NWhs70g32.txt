(CNN) Texas police are looking for an Uber passenger who shot and killed another rider Friday near Houston, authorities said.Harris County Sheriff Ed Gonzalez identified the victim as James Grant Booker II, 26. The alleged shooter is on the run, and police are searching the surrounding areas.The Uber driver called police after attempting to pick up two passengers at an intersection, Gonzalez said.One of the two men sat in the rear passenger's side of the SUV, while the other came around the back and then fired "multiple times" into the back seat where "his companion" was, Gonzalez said.The driver left with the injured man and called police.
