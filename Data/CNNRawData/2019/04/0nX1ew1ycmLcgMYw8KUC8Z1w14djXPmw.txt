(CNN) Police in Minnesota are investigating the deaths of two people at a home owned by former Minnesota Vikings minority owner Irwin Jacobs.Orono Police said the bodies of a man and a woman were discovered at a home in Orono, Minnesota. Police said the two were found dead and that there was a handgun found on the bed.Authorities have not disclosed their identities.Property records show the mansion is owned by businessman Irwin Jacobs and his wife Alexandra, CNN affiliate WCCO reported.The Hennepin County Dispatch center got the call to respond to the scene at 8:31 a.m. on Wednesday, and police said there is no risk to the public.This aerial photo shows Irwin Jacobs' home in Orono, Minnesota on Wednesday, April 20, 2019.Read More
