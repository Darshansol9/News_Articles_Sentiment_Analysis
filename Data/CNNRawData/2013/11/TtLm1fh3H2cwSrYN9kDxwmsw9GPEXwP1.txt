Story highlights A 10-year-old zebra bites a zookeeper multiple timesThe keeper was taken to a hospitalA gazelle dies, probably after being spooked by the eventsA 10-year-old zebra at the National Zoo in Washington bit a staff member several times Monday morning, according to zoo officials.The injured zookeeper was alert and talking after the incident, but was taken to a hospital as zoo officials continued to investigate what happened.They said the incident apparently led to the death of a young gazelle, which probably was spooked by the biting zebra. A necropsy report shows fractured vertebrae that could have been sustained when the year-old gazelle, named Tony, ran into a barrier.The biting zebra, known as Gumu, was unharmed and has been taken out of the zoo's zebra exhibit.
