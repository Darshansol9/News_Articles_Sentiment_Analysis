Where's the line between accommodation and discrimination? At what point is a child old enough for that to even be an issue?The case focuses on Coy Mathis, a 6-year-old born with a boy's body. She identifies as a girl, and her family is raising her as a girl.In kindergarten, she used the girl's bathroom with no problem, the family says.
