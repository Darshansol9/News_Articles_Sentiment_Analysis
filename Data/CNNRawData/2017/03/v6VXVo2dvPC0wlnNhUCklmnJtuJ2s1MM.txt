And then another.A construction crew working on a new building in Philadelphia on Thursday uncovered dozens of coffins buried 6 feet below a parking lot.The site of a new apartment complex has been temporarily transformed into a quest to unearth the city's past. Archaeologists are sorting through the remains, hoping to glean a better idea of what life was like in colonial Philadelphia.Historians say the coffins were part of the First Baptist Church burial ground established in 1707 in the historic part of the city on what is now Arch Street.
