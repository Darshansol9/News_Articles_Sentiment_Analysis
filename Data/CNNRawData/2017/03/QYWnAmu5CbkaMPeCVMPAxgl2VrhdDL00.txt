(CNN) What do you do when you're a student and you want to compete in robotics competitions -- and you're blind? Well, you go to the dark side, of course.Dark Side is the name of a robotics team in Iowa made up mostly of blind students. It's taking part in a regional competition this weekend in Cedar Falls against dozens of other teams from the US, China and Brazil.One of the team members said he asked teachers if they could form a team because they had something to prove."We can do it and we can pass it down to other visually-impaired students.
