(CNN) Gonzaga will play in its first NCAA men's basketball title game after edging South Carolina 77-73 on Saturday in the national semifinals."I'm really, really proud of our guys and our late-game execution," Gonzaga Coach Mark Few said. "The guys executed it perfectly, especially down the last four minutes. ...
