Story highlights Maryland's Washington College put on lockdown due to a potential gun threatStudents and staff advised to shelter in place; visitors have been advised to stay away(CNN) The campus of Washington College, a university in Chestertown, Maryland, was closed Monday due to a possible gun threat, the school announced on its website.In a statement posted Monday morning , school authorities said they had been informed by the parents of a sophomore student that he had gone home and retrieved a gun."The parents have not been able to reach him and do not know where he is or where he is headed," the statement said.Out of "an abundance of caution," all students and staff should shelter in place, school officials said, while others should stay away from the campus.Local law enforcement officials were on campus and sweeps of the college were underway, the statement said.Read More
