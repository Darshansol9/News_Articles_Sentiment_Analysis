Story highlights Train conductor in Connecticut got the chance to conduct a glee clubThe video went viral soon after Bob McDonough posted it on his Facebook page(CNN) It was anything but a normal day for train conductor Bob McDonough.The Stamford, Connecticut, man, who has been on the job for 30 years, encountered the Yale Glee Club aboard his train on Friday night.He asked the group to sing so he could literally conduct them. The group sang "Carol of the Bells."McDonough posted the video, shot by two passengers on the train, on Facebook. It soon went viral."I post it and you can see the counter, beep beep beep, going up like the stock market, it was crazy," McDonough told CNN affiliate WTICRead More
