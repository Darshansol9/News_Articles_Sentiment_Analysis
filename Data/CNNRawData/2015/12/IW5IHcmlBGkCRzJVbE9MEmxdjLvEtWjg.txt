There were no patients aboard the aircraft."We are deeply saddened by the news that two of our crew members were taken from us, and our hearts go out to their family and loved ones," the company said in a statement.The crash occurred near Apache Junction, Brodsly said. The town is located on the eastern edge of Phoenix.The crew members included a pilot, a flight paramedic and a flight nurse.
