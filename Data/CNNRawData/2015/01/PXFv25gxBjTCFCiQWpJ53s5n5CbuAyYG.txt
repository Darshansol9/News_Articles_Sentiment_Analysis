Brendan Ryan.The 38-year-old manager, who was shot in an aisle of the store, was transported to Bellevue Hospital where he was later pronounced dead, Ryan said.The shooting happened in a store at 40 West 23rd Street, in the Flatiron section of Manhattan, police said.The shooter, a 31-year-old male worker at the store, was not scheduled to work when he came in, Ryan said.
