(CNN) Phil "Flip" Saunders, head coach of the NBA's Minnesota Timberwolves, has died, the team announced Sunday.Flip Saunders gestures during a game on March 9 in Los Angeles.Saunders, who also served as the team's president of basketball operations and part owner, was 60.Saunders is credited with leading the Timberwolves to some of the team's most successful seasons during his tenure as coach from 1995 to 2005.
