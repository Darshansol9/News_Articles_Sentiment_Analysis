(CNN) Spring is in the air and underwater -- especially off the coast of British Columbia.Four orca babies have been born in the last three months, a higher number than usual, according to the Center for Whale Research.Orcas in the area are called the Southern Residents and this baby boom brings their total to 81 whales."We hope this is a sustained upswing in the population, but they are endangered and will remain endangered until we can get their population up over 100," said Ken Balcomb with the Center for Whale Research.The first calf of the season was spotted in December, followed by two in February and this last one in March.Read More
