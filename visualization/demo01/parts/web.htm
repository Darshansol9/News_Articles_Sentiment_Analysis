News Articles Sentiment Analysis

This project helps us find the trend in the US over the years with the type of news published by legit sources like CNN to make inferences about the things happening, are they more pessimists or positive progress in the country. We could also make inferences about the number of negative and positive news over the years has not been a consistent ratio and it’s variably changing. We have used several algorithms to not influence the bias in the articles and give sentiments to the articles in the most accurate way. Graphical Representations has been incorporated for end results in a visual way.
